VIC 博士类别：

对于澳大利亚的博士毕业生，毕业没超过5年，且所研究专业与上面列表里的职业高度相关，则不需要相关工作经验，只要雅思4个6就可以申请VIC州担保。

VIC 州担保对某些职业的特殊要求

231212 Ships Engineer (Marine Engineer) 要求 Class One and Class Two engineers required.
252411 Occupational Therapist 要求 Mental Health

对于 272511 Social Worker 优先考虑如下类别：
Mental Health Workers
Skilled Children and Family Workers
Child Protection (Must have a minimum of two years statutory child protection practice and Australian Association of Social Workers accreditation).

中学教师（241411 Secondary School Teacher）需要教授下面一门课程：
Mathematics or Physics
Technology (Food, Metal, Wood or Auto)
Languages
General science
Special education
Information technology.

职业教育老师（242211 Vocational Education Teacher）需要是如下类别：
Must specialise in at least one of the following fields: Horticulture
Arboriculture
Building and design
Dogger and Rigger (Crane Licenses)
Electrical Instrumentation
Electrical Supply Industry Transmission, Distribution and Cable Jointing
Gas Distribution
Aged Care

口译（272412 Interpreter）需要通过NAATI认证。
Must have NAATI “professional” accreditation.

此外，当前VIC不担保如下ACS职业：
Web Designer
Help Desk Operator
Help Desk Manager.

最新澳大利亚EOI后技术移民类别（SkillSelect）：

189 独立技术移民永久签证 - Subclass 189 Skilled – Independent (Permanent) ；
190 州担保技术移民永久签证 - Subclass 190 Skilled – Nominated (Permanent) ；
489 地区担保技术移民永久签证 - Subclass 489 Skilled – Regional Nominated or Sponsored (Provisional) ；
