魁北克技术移民2009年最新政策及变化趋势

加拿大移民部2008年推出了加拿大经验类移民（Canadian Experience Class），魁北克移民部将在今年对魁北克技术移民体系进行改革，来更快的吸收魁北克留学生、在魁北克合法工作的外国劳动者（Quebec adopts special immigration program for international students and foreign workers,2009.07 执行），同时魁北克移民部推出了新的优先处理受训领域列表来加快吸收本地劳动力市场紧缺职业申请者（2009.10 执行）。魁北克移民部有可能将通过简化申请程序，给特殊类别申请者优先处理的政策来加快这类移民的速度。以下分别介绍各类别大致申请条件，可以看出法语在魁北克移民申请者占有重要地位。

一、 魁北克的国际留学生类（International Students）

1. 国际留学生必须获取了高中后的职业教育文凭，在学院学习获得技术课程文凭，或者在大学或科研机构获得魁北克认可的学士学位，硕士学位或博士学位。
To qualify for a CSQ, international students must have obtained a secondary school vocational diploma, a diploma of college studies in a technical program, or a university diploma attesting to a bachelor's degree, a master's degree, or a doctorate from an educational institution recognized by the Quebec Ministry of Education.

2. 法语很重要：必须完成了法语课程 或者 至少在高中后学校完成了一年以上全日制的法语学习 或者 在魁北克教育机构成功完成了 B1 级别的法语课程。
To be recognized for Quebec immigration, these studies must have been completed in French or the applicants must have completed at least one year of full-time studies in French at the secondary or post-secondary level or have successfully completed a level B1 French course at a Quebec educational institution.

3. 符合以上条件的申请人离开魁北克不超过24个月时可以在境外申请该类别。
Candidates may apply from abroad if they have obtained their diploma in Quebec no more than 24 months before the new regulations come into effect.

二、 临时工人（Specialized temporary workers）

1. 身份：在魁北克合法工作，在递交申请前的24个月内必须在魁北克某一技术职业全职工作至少12个月以上。
To qualify for this fast-track CSQ program, foreign workers must be lawfully in Quebec at the time of application submission. They must have had full-time employment in Quebec in a skilled occupation for a period of at least 12 months in the 24 months preceding the date of application.

2．法语很重要：至少接受了高中或高中后一年以上全日制法语学习 或者 在魁北克教育机构完成了 B1 级别的法语课程。或者，能够提供适合当前 professional order 的法语能力，或者 提供标准的法语考试成绩，例如TEF。
Foreign worker applicants are also subject to a French language requirement. They must have pursued full-time studies for at least one year at the secondary or post-secondary level, or have taken a level B1 French course at a Quebec educational institution. Alternatively, an applicant can submit documentation with their application, certifying that they meet the French language requirements of a professional order, or that they have received satisfactory results on a standardized French language test.

三、 紧缺职业类别（occupations that are short of workers）

魁北克劳动力市场紧缺职业的申请者将会得到快速处理。当前魁北克政府发布了优先处理受训领域列表（List of Preferred Areas of Training），2009-10-14 后如果申请者的专业在该列表（在申请前5年内取得的）将优先处理。
Quebec immigration applicants whose profiles correspond to the province's occupations that are short of workers: Qualified Quebec immigration applicants who have training/experience in occupations that are considered in high-demand by the province of Quebec will have their immigration applications fast-tracked. Quebec's labour market is in need of applicants at all skills levels, from welders to laboratory technicians, to nurses, and aeronautical engineers. Applicants with training/experience in Quebec's in-demand occupations have the best employment prospects and will therefore receive priority processing in their CSQ applications.

Quebec Immigration has issued a "List of Preferred Areas of Training". As of October 14, 2009, qualified applicants who have received a diploma listed on this document in the past five years before submitting their application will receive priority processing.
