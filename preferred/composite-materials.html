复合材料应用 Use of composite materials - Secondary level vocational training

在魁省教育体系里，该科目要求完成900个学时的二级职业训练，总教育年限为11年。
In the Québec education system, completion of this program requires 900 hours of secondary level vocational training, representing a total of 11 years of study.

该专业具有很好就业前景的职业是：塑料制品装配员，处理员，检查员 Plastic products assembler, finisher and inspector (NOC 9495)。

9495 塑料制品装配员，处理员，检查员 主要职责：

该职业人员负责安装，处理并检查塑料件及其成品。受雇于塑料制品生产商和飞机等制造行业的塑料件部门。在魁省，该职业是不受规范的。
Plastic products assemblers, finishers and inspectors assemble, finish and inspect plastic parts and finished products.They are employed by plastic products manufacturing companies and plastic parts divisions of aircraft or other manufacturing companies.In Québec, this occupation is not regulated.
