国外无犯罪之荷兰无犯罪记录证明 - FLYabroad

荷兰无犯罪记录证明的办理机构是荷兰司法行政部（MINISTRY OF JUSTICE），办理周期 2 - 4 周，费用 €30.05 欧元；荷兰无犯罪证明是荷兰文的，但有英语摘要（We will issue a Dutch certificate with an English summary）。

申请表格

荷兰无犯罪证明申请很简单，首先下载申请表格（英文版）和申请指南（英文版），荷兰文版请去网站下载。根据指南申请就可以。

一般情况下，申请表上只填写个人信息部分就可以，机构信息部分不需要自己填。（The section '2. Data organisation/establishment' has to be filled out by the department that requires the certificate. If they are unable to do this, an official letter or brochure which states that you have to apply for a certificate of good behaviour will do as well.）

付费

荷兰无犯罪的办理费用是 35 欧元，付款信息（Bank data）：
IBAN- CODE: nl80 rabo 0192327984
BIC CODE (swift code): rabonl2u
rekeningno. (accountnr): 192327984
bank name in full: Rabobank International, Croeselaan 18, 3521 CB Utrecht, Postbus 17100, 3500 HG Utrecht
made payable to the Ministerie van Justitie/Dienst JUSTIS/COVOG in Den Haag stating the name of the applicant.

递交申请

申请表填写完整后，将申请表（ your applicationform）附上付费单据（a copy of the payment ）、护照复印件（a copy of your ID card or passport,）及办理用途的材料（例如：使馆要求的补料信）递交到如下地址：
Ministerie van Justitie
Afdeling Covog
Postbus 16115
2500 BC DEN HAAG
The Netherlands
