234511 生命科学家（普通）职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad

研究有机体的起源，解剖学，生理学，繁殖和行为，并研究他们与所生活的环境相作用的方式。 Studies the origin, anatomy, physiology, reproduction and behaviour of living organisms and the ways in which they interact with the environment in which they live.
