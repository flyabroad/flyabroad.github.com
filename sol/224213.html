224213 卫生信息管理员职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad

卫生信息管理员规划、开发、实现和管理卫生信息服务,如患者信息系统，临床和管理数据,以满足医疗服务对医疗、法律、伦理和管理卫生保健的要求。 Plans, develops, implements and manages health information services, such as patient information systems, and clinical and administrative data, to meet the medical, legal, ethical and administrative requirements of health care delivery.
