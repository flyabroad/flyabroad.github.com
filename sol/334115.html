334115 屋顶水暖工职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad

屋顶水暖工安装，维护和修理防水板、金属屋顶、墙面表层和防水产品，如水槽和落水管。需要注册或许可。 Installs, maintains and repairs flashings, metallic roof and wall claddings and rainwater products such as gutters and downpipes. Registration or licensing is required.

Previously referred to in ASCO as: 4431-17 Roof Plumber
