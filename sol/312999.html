312999 建筑及工程技术员执业注册要求（不代表移民要求） - FLYabroad

Registration or licensing may be required.

New South Wales - Licensing is required for all plumbing/draining/electrical/air conditioning/refrigeration work in New South Wales.

Victoria - Registration is compulsory for Building Surveyors, Building Inspector, Quantity Surveyors, Fire Safety officers (if working in the building industry and approving designs), Draftspersons, Commercial Builders, Domestic Builders, Demolishers, Building Erectors, or Building Supervisors.

Queensland - Licensing is compulsory in order to carry out or supervise building work over the value of AUD3300 (including Labour and materials).

Northern Territory - Registration is compulsory in order to carry out residential building work over the value of AUD12 000.

Australian Capital Territory - Licensing is compulsory.
