234314 护林员职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad

护林员负责依据授权的政策和优先级，帮助控制州际或国家公园，风景区，历史遗迹，自然资源，游乐场所或者保育休耕。 Assists in controlling a State or national park, scenic area, historic site, nature reserve, recreation area or conservation reserve in accordance with authorised policies and priorities.

Previously referred to in ASCO as: 2114-15 Park Ranger
