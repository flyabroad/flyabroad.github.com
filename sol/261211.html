261211 多媒体专家职业描述 Job description - FLYabroad

多媒体专家创建和操纵电脑动画、音频、视频和图形图像文件，变为多媒体程序，为光盘、信息亭、多媒体演示、网站、手机资源、电子游戏环境、电子商务、电子安全解决方案以及环境和教育教育产品，制作数据和内容。 Creates and manipulates computer animation, audio, video and graphic image files into multimedia programs to produce data and content for CD-ROMs, information kiosks, multimedia presentations, websites, mobile telephone resources, electronic gaming environments, e-commerce and e-security solutions, and entertainment and education products.
