加拿大酒店服务员清洁工（Hotel Attendant and CLEANING WORKER）招工信息 - 工作内容，工作待遇，工作要求

工作地点：BC 省 和 Alberta 省，加拿大

职位：酒店服务员和清洁工（Hotel Attendant and CLEANING WORKER）

工作描述

责任- 清洗、打扫、整理地板、地毯，清扫窗帘和家具灰尘，布置毛巾，洗浴用品，浴室清洁消毒及固定装置，清理垃圾桶，洗涤窗户，墙壁和天花板。
服务范围：酒店，温泉度假村，旅馆，酒店和汽车旅馆

工时、工资、福利

-不提供住宿
-不提供工作餐
-40 小时/周（可能要加班）
-医疗保险（自我支付） w cb（工人赔偿委员会保险所涵盖的雇主;工作伤害）
-工资： 1,920加币\月（ 20天） 相当于 12加币/每小时（以合同为准）
-合同期限 12-24 个月

职位要求

•高中毕业以上学历。
•能够通过健康体检和背景检查（无犯罪记录）
•至少有2至3年的工作经验
•年龄不超过40
•中文或英文流利

签证种类

工作签证证+多次入境签证

原英文招聘内容：

JOB ORDER

 

Location :      B.C. and Alberta, Canada

 

Job Title     :        Hotel Attendant and CLEANING WORKER

 

Job Descriptions

 

Responsibilities- Sweep, mop and wash floors, Wax and polish floors, Dust furniture, Vacuum carpeting, area rugs, draperies and upholstered furniture, Distribute clean towels and toiletries, Clean and disinfect bathrooms and fixtures, Pick up debris and empty trash containers, Wash windows, walls and ceilings.

 

Services: Hotels, SPA resorts, Lodges, Inn and Motel

 

 

Working Hours & Wages & Benefits

 

-          No Housing

-          No meal/working day

-          40h/week + possible over time

-          Medical Insurance (self-paid) WCB (worker compensation board insurance covered by the employer; working injury)

-          Salary CAD$1,920 per month (20 days) @$12/ per hour

*Subject to the contract.

-          12-24 months contracts

 

 

Requirement

 

¨           High School Diploma. 

¨           Able to pass drug test and background check (no criminal record)

¨           At least 2 to 3 years working experiences

¨           Age not more than 40

¨           Fluent speaking in intermediate spoke in English or Chinese

 

Visa Type

Working permit + multiple entrance Visa
