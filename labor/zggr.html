加拿大摘菇工人招工信息 - 工作内容，工作待遇，工作要求

JOB ORDER (FREE JOB OFFER)-可自行打工

Location/地點    :      B.C., Alberta, Canada
Job Title/工作职位 : 摘菇工人

Job Descriptions/工作内容

•    Plant, fertilize, cultivate, spray, irrigate and harvest crops
种植,施肥,培育,喷洒,灌溉和收获作物

•    Operate and maintain farm machinery and equipment
使用和维修农用机械设备

•    Detect disease and health problems in crops, livestock and poultry
检查农作物,牲畜,家禽的疾病及健康问题

•    Examine produce for quality and prepare for market
检查产品质量为市场做准备

•    Set and monitor water lines, air flow and temperature in barns, pens and chicken coops
在谷仓,畜圈,鸡笼里设定和监测水线,空气流通,温度

•    Clean stables, barns, barnyards and pens.
清扫畜栏,谷仓,谷仓空场,畜圈.

•    General farm workers can become specialized in a particular type of crop or livestock production through experience.
一般的农场工人可以通过一些对农作物或者牲畜生产经验成为特定的农业工人.

Working Hours & Wages & Benefits/工资待遇

-    40h/week + possible over time/工作时间每周40小时
-    Medical Insurance (self-paid) WCB (worker compensation board insurance covered by the employer; working injury)/工作伤害险
-    Salary CAD$1,600 per month (20 days) @$11/per hour + Over time *1.5
    *Subject to the contract./ 每月1,600加币 (可自行打工)
-    24 months contracts/24月合约

Requirement /工作要求

•    Gender: Male only /男, 女性
•    High School Diploma. /高中毕业
•    Able to pass drug test and background check (no criminal record) /无罪, 无吸毒
•    At least 2 to 3 years working experiences /2-3相关工作经验
•    Age not more than 40/40岁以下
•    Fluent speaking in intermediate spoke in English or French/会英文

Visa Type/工作签证

Working permit + multiple entrance Visa
