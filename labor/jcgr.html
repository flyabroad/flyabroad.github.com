加拿大鸡场工人招工信息 - 工作内容，工作待遇，工作要求

JOB ORDER  鸡场工人

Location/地點    :      B.C., Surrey, Canada
Job Title/工作职位 : 鸡场工人

Job Descriptions/工作内容

•    Industrial butchers slaughter livestock using stunning devices and knives; skin, clean and trim carcasses; remove viscera and other inedible parts from carcasses; split carcasses into smaller portions to facilitate handling; and may slaughter cattle, calves and sheep as prescribed by religious laws.
需要使用极好的设备和刀; 去皮,清洗及整理畜体; 从畜体中移除内脏及不宜食的部分; 将畜体分割成小的部分以便处理; 将根据宗教法律对鸡进行屠宰

•    Industrial meat cutters cut beef, lamb, pork or veal carcasses or sides or quarters of carcasses into primal cuts for further cutting, processing or packaging; remove bones from meat; and cut meat and poultry into specific cuts for institutional, commercial or other wholesale use.
要把鸡或者犊鸡的切成小部分以便未来进一步的切割,加工以及包装; 把骨头从肉中剔除并根据工业,商业以及其他批发的特殊要求切割肉类.

•    Poultry preparers slaughter poultry and remove inedible parts; remove feathers and singe and wash poultry to prepare for further processing or packaging.
家禽类要把不宜食的部分剔除;把羽毛烧掉剔除以便未来的加工及包装.

Working Hours & Wages & Benefits/工资待遇

-    40h/week + possible over time/工作时间每周40小时
-    Medical Insurance (self-paid) WCB (worker compensation board insurance covered by the employer; working injury)/工作伤害险
-    Salary CAD$2,080 per month (20 days) @$13/per hour + Over time *1.5
    *Subject to the contract./ 每月2,080加币
-    24 months contracts/24月合约

Requirement /工作要求

•    Gender: Male only /男(打扫清洗), Female 女性 (手小去鸡皮,清洗及整理畜体; 从畜体中移除内脏及不宜食的部分)
•    High School Diploma. /高中毕业
•    Able to pass drug test and background check (no criminal record) /无罪, 无吸毒
•    At least 2 to 3 years working experiences /2-3相关工作经验
•    Age not more than 40/40岁以下

Visa Type/工作签证
Working permit + multiple entrance Visa
