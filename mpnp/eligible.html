曼省技术移民申请条件

参考：FLYabroad 论坛 ；   修改时间： 2008-11-1 ；  站内搜索：      FLYabroad 免费评估！free!

曼省技术移民主要对申请者的如下方面进行评估

* 教育和培训（education and training）
* 工作经验（work experience）
* 英语或法语能力（enough English or French to start a job soon after arrival）
* 与曼省的联系程度（a connection to Manitoba (for example, friends or family or current employment)）
* 定居资金（settlement supports (enough money to maintain yourself and your family while you are becoming established)）
* 到曼省的定居意向（the intention to settle permanently in Manitoba）

申请曼省提名技术移民的基本条件

1. 合法居民身份，在所在国的合法居住状态，如果当前在加拿大，必须提供您的访问，学生或临时工作的证明。
2. 必须证明您到曼省后有很强的就业能力，例如您的培训经历，执照证书，工作经验，语言能力等。
3. 有足够的资金来支付各种申请费用及定居费用。
4. 根据自身情况判断适合哪种类别的技术移民，然后整理所有的表格和材料然后递交申请。

曼省技术移民类别及相应申请条件

一、优先评估类别（PRIORITY ASSESSMENT STREAM）

1. 雇主驱动类（Employer Direct）
• 通过 MPNP 核准的曼省长期全职工作机会（an offer of a long-term, full-time job in Manitoba pre-approved by the MPNP）
如果该工作机会（job offer）没有得到 MPNP 的核准（pre-approved），则必须：
• 有一份曼省提供的长期全职工作机会（have an offer of a long-term, full-time job in Manitoba）
• 持有 CIC 签发的工作许可（have a work permit issued by Citizenship and Immigration Canada）
• 已经为该雇主工作 6 个月以上（be currently working for the Manitoba employer that has offered you the job and been working there for at least six months）

2. 国际留学生类（International Student）
• 一份曼省的长期全职工作 Offer（an offer of a long-term, full-time job in Manitoba）
• 持有毕业生工作签证（a post-graduate open work permit issued by Citizenship and Immigration Canada after you completed a post-secondary educational program of at least eight months in Manitoba）
• 为该雇主工作 6 个月以上（currently working for the Manitoba employer that offered you the job and been working there for at least six months）

3. 家庭支持类（Family Support）
• 在曼省有近亲愿意对你进行担保（a close relative in Manitoba who has signed a Manitoba Affidavit of Support (MAS)）
• 申请者达到了申请家庭担保移民的最低条件（meet the minimum criteria for the Family Support stream）

如果申请者不能满足上面优先类别的要求，可以考虑如下普通类别（If your application is not eligible for consideration under a priority stream you may be eligible to apply under the General stream）

二、普通类别（General stream）

满足如下条件之一可申请 General Stream：
• 在曼省有近亲并且愿意进行担保宣誓（a close relative in Manitoba who has signed an Affidavit of Support (MAS)）
• 曼省的两个朋友或者两个远亲愿意对你进行担保（two friends or distant relatives in Manitoba who have each signed an affidavit of support）
• 在曼省完成了教育（completed education in Manitoba）
• 曾经在曼省工作过 6 个月以上（previous work experience in Manitoba (six months minimum)）

对于普通类别，您需要进行分数评估，总分 106 分，及格 55 分。

三、战略方案类（Strategic Initiatives）

MPNP 根据程序需要和现有资源可以对申请者进行自由裁决，该类别申请资格：（Strategic Initiatives are undertaken at the discretion of the MPNP in accordance with program needs and available resources. You are eligible to apply if you）
• 参加了被 MPNP 批准的曼省的考察访问并且与 MPNP 官员进行了会面，或者（participated in an exploratory visit to Manitoba, confirmed by the MPNP, and had an interview with a MPNP officer, or）
• 与 MPNP 官员会面后收到了针对 MPNP 的海外宣传倡议申请的邀请信，或者（were interviewed by a MPNP officer and received a letter of invitation to apply as part of a MPNP targeted overseas promotional initiative, or）
• 曼省的民族文化社团安排你对曼省进行考察访问并且该访问被 MPNP 核准（have the support of an ethnocultural community organization in Manitoba that will help you arrange an exploratory visit that has been pre-approved by the MPNP）
