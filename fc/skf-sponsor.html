萨省（Saskatchewan）省提名家庭成员担保移民担保人（Supporting Family Member）担保条件

如果您在萨省（Saskatchewan）有下列一个或多个亲属，并且该亲属在萨省连续生活一年以上，你可以考虑申请 Saskatchewan 的家庭类移民（Family Members）。这些亲属包括：
parents（父母）， nieces/nephews（侄子侄女，外甥外甥女） ， daughters/sons（子女）， first cousins（第一代堂兄弟姐妹）， sisters/brothers（兄弟姐妹），grandchildren（孙子孙女，外孙外孙女）， aunts/uncles（姑姨，叔舅） ， grandparents（祖父母，外祖父母） ，step-family members / in - laws of the same relationship （同等的继亲关系或者姻亲关系）。

step- or half- relative 等亲属关系定义，可以参考 加拿大家庭成员定义（Family Member Definitions）。

当然要想担保海外亲属移民加拿大（萨省），担保人还需要满足下列要求：

1. 担保人在进行担保时是加拿大公民或者永久居民
Be a Canadian citizen or permanent resident of Canada at the time that your application is submitted to the SINP;

2. 证明萨省是担保人的主要居住地
Prove that Saskatchewan has been their principal residence;

3. 证明担保人与申请人的亲属关系
Prove their genealogical family relationship to you;

4. 担保人具有财政自足能力，过去六个月内没有接受萨省救济
Be financially self-supporting (have not received financial support through the Saskatchewan Assistance Plan, Employment Insurance, or the Resettlement Assistance Program within the last six months); and

5. 如果要为申请者提供定居资金，最少提供保证申请者 3 个月的花费（主申请 $10,000 加币，每增加一个随行者增加 $2,000 加币）。
If providing settlement funds for you, show they have had possession of the funds for at least three months.

当申请萨省省提名家庭成员担保移民（SINP Family Members Category）时，申请者必须指明你的担保人（或其配偶）是否已经担保过其他 SINP 申请人。
When you apply to the SINP Family Members Category, you must indicate if your supporting family member (or your supporting family member’s spouse) is already supporting other applicants to the SINP.

注意：对于 萨省 SINP 家庭成员担保移民，SINP 会将所有通信信件发给担保人。
Under the SINP Family Members Category, your supporting family member will receive all written correspondence from the SINP to you. Your supporting family member is therefore responsible for making sure that you receive all information concerning your application.
