纽芬兰团聚类移民（NLPNP Family Connections Category)申请流程

参考：FLYabroad 论坛 ；   修改时间： 2008-8-3 ；  站内搜索：      FLYabroad 免费评估！free!

与其他加拿大省提名移民类似，纽芬兰家庭关系类移民的申请流程主要分为两阶段（Two-Step Immigration Process），第一步是申请 Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program (NLPNP) 的提名资格，第二步是申请 NOC 移民签证。

第一步：向 NLPNP 办公室申请纽芬兰家庭关系类移民（NLPNP Family Connections Category)的提名资格，期间涉及到准备大量的申请表格和辅助材料。

材料准备的基本流程（由于很简单，不提供翻译和解释，FLYabroad 注）：
Step 1: Print off this Application Guide for the Family Connection Category.
Step 2: Read the Application Guide and the instructions before completing your application.
Step 3: Print off all the required forms associated with this category.
Step 4: Obtain all the required supporting documents and make photocopies of them.
Step 5: Have your Sponsor complete all the Sponsor‐related forms and supporting documents.
Step 6: Read and complete the required forms.
Step 7: Review and organize your completed forms and supporting documents as outlined in the Family Connection Category Checklist.
Step 8: Photocopy all your completed forms and supporting documents. Keep the photocopies for your records.
Step 9: Prepare your cheque or bank draft for the sum of $150.00 CAD and make it payable to “Newfoundland Exchequer”.

材料准备齐全后邮寄到 NLPNP 提名处理中心，邮寄地址：
Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism
Provincial Nominee program
P.O. Box 8700
St. John’s AIB 4J6
Newfoundland
CANADA

NLPNP 收到提名申请后将会有准备的签证官对提名资格进行具体的评估：
• NLPNP 根据申请材料决定是否通过申请者的提名，期间有可能会会与担保人进行多次的电话、传真或信件的沟通
• 如果申请者的提名资格被通过，NLPNP 处理中心将：
        o 将提名证明及相关表格及说明发给担保人（Your federal forms, supporting documents, and a letter of nomination will be sent to the Sponsor.）；同时
        o 同时将递交联邦移民签证申请的说明发给担保人（The Sponsor will also receive detailed instructions on sending your application to the appropriate Canadian visa office.）

第二步：将提名证明向 CIC 申请签证（Apply to CIC with your SINP nomination）
• CIC 审理您省提名移民签证（CIC considers your application after they receive the nomination certificate from the SINP）
• CIC 进行体检，背景及安全调查，发放签证（CIC does health, security and criminal reviews, and then issues visas to you and your family members）

有问题可以通过电话、传真或者邮件进行查询，NLPNP 的具体联系方式：

电话: 1‐709‐729‐6607
传真: 1‐709‐729‐7381
邮件: info@nlpnp.ca
网站: www.nlpnp.ca

申请人条件：纽芬兰家庭关系类移民（NLPNP Family Connections Category)申请条件（申请人资格）。
担保人条件：纽芬兰家庭关系类移民（NLPNP Family Connections Category)担保条件（担保人资格）。
