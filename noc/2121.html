2121 加拿大生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家职责概述-FLYabroad

生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家进行基础研究和应用研究以扩大有机生物体的知识，管理自然资源并开发新的与医学和农学相关的产品。生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家受雇于实验室和由政府设立的生态环境顾问公司，公共事业公司，化学、药物、生物工艺公司以及健康教育机构。 Biologists and related scientists conduct basic and applied research to extend knowledge of living organisms, to manage natural resources, and to develop new practices and products related to medicine and agriculture. They are employed in both laboratory and field settings by governments, environmental consulting companies, resource and utilities companies, chemical, pharmaceutical and biotechnical companies and health and educational institutions.

2121 加拿大生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家的主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

A 生物学家履行以下部分或者全部职责： Biologists perform some or all of the following duties:

• 计划并研究环境、人口的分配、结构和功能特点以及动植物的行为习性 Plan and conduct studies of the environment, and of the population, distribution, structure and functional characteristics and behaviour of plants and animals
• 进行生态环境影响的研究，并撰写相关的报告 Conduct ecological and environmental impact studies and prepare reports
• 研究，识别并为动植物标本分类 Study, identify and classify plant and animal specimens
• 对动植物的生长，遗传和繁殖进行实验 Conduct experiments in plant or animal growth, heredity and breeding
• 对可再生资源的管理准备报告并制定计划 Prepare reports and plans for management of renewable resources
• 可能管理生物技术员，技术专家和其他科学家 May supervise biological technologists and technicians and other scientists.

B 微生物学家、细胞生物学家和分子结构生物学家履行以下部分或者全部职责： Microbiologists and cell and molecular biologists perform some or all of the following duties:

• 研究微生物的结构、功能、生态学、生物技术以及遗传学，这些微生物包括细菌、真菌、原生动物和藻类 Conduct research into the structure, function, ecology, biotechnology and genetics of micro-organisms, including bacteria, fungi, protozoans, and algae
• 研究人类，动物和植物的组织细胞的结构和功能 Conduct research into the structure and functioning of human, animal and plant tissues and cells
• 研究人类，植物和动物的病原体和毒素的识别，影响以及控制 Conduct studies into the identification, effects and control of human, plant and animal pathogens and toxins
• 进行临床和实验室研究，以测试、评估并筛选毒品和药物 Conduct clinical or laboratory studies to test, evaluate and screen drugs and pharmaceuticals
• 对基因表达，基因操作和DNA重组技术进行分子和生物化学的研究及实验 Conduct molecular or biochemical studies and experiments into genetic expression, gene manipulation and recombinant DNA technology
• 进行研究以发现、开发、改善并评估新产品 Conduct research to discover, develop and refine, and evaluate new products
• 可能参加产品的商业化 May participate in the commercialization of new products
• 可能负责管理生物学技术专家、生物学技术人员和其他科学家 May supervise biological technologists and technicians and other scientists
• 可能用电脑建模技术进行生物统计学数据分析 May conduct biostatistical data analysis using computer modelling techniques.
• 生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家可能专门从事宏观领域的研究，如植物学、动物学、生态和海洋生物学以及细胞和分子层次的研究，如遗传学、免疫学、药理学、毒理学、生理学、病理学、细菌学和病毒学 Biologists and related scientists may specialize at the macroscopic level, in fields such as botany, zoology, ecology and marine biology or, at the cellular and molecular level, in fields such as genetics, immunology, pharmacology, toxicology, physiology, pathology, bacteriology and virology.

2121 加拿大生物学家及生物学相关类别的科学家的就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 生物学家要求生物学或者相关学科的学士学位A bachelor's degree in biology or in a related discipline is required for biologists.
• 生物研究科学家要求生物学或者相关学科的硕士学位A master's or doctoral degree in biology or a related discipline is required for employment as a research scientist in biology.
• 学术部门或研究机构通常要求需要有博士后研究经验Post-doctoral research experience is usually required before employment in academic departments or research institutions.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民的紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
