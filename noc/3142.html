3142 加拿大物理治疗师的职责概述-FLYabroad

物理治疗师需要评估病人的病情，并制定和帮助病人完成针对个人的治疗方案，以此来保持、提高或恢复病人的生理功能、减轻他们的痛苦、预防躯体功能障碍。 Physiotherapists assess patients and plan and carry out individually designed treatment programs to maintain, improve or restore physical functioning, alleviate pain and prevent physical dysfunction in patients. Physiotherapists are employed in hospitals, clinics, industry, sports organizations, rehabilitation centres and extended care facilities, or they may work in private practice.

3142 加拿大物理治疗师的头衔范例 Example Titles-FLYabroad

• 研究型的物理治疗师 research physiotherapist

3142 加拿大物理治疗师的主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

物理治疗师应履行下列部分或全部职责： Physiotherapists perform some or all of the following duties:

• 通过评估程序来评估病人的体能，如功能能力测试 Assess patients' physical abilities through evaluative procedures such as functional ability tests
• 在物理诊断的基础上，给病人制定治疗目标 Establish treatment goals with patients based on physical diagnoses
• 计划并实施物理治疗方案，包括运动疗法、按摩、教育、使用电疗仪和其他机械设备以及水疗法 Plan and implement programs of physiotherapy including therapeutic exercise, manipulations, massage, education, the use of electro-therapeutic and other mechanical equipment and hydro-therapy
• 评估治疗效果，并做相应的修改 Evaluate effectiveness of treatment plans and modify accordingly
• 针对病人出现的问题、需求和进展情况，同咨询医师和其他医疗专业人士进行沟通和交流 Communicate with referring physician and other healthcare professionals regarding patients' problems, needs and progress
• 整理临床统计记录并和其他医护专业人员交换意见 Maintain clinical and statistical records and confer with other health care professionals
• 为病人、工作人员和社区群众制定并实施健康促进计划 Develop and implement health promotion programs for patients, staff and the community
• 可能会进行理疗方面的研究 May conduct research in physiotherapy
• 可能需要提供咨询或教育服务 May provide consulting or education services.
• 物理理疗师可能会集中在这几个方面进行实践，尤其是临床领域，如神经科、肿瘤科、风湿病科、骨科、妇产科、儿科、老年病学等；还有心血管病和心肺疾病方面、烧伤、运动伤害或人机工程学领域 Physiotherapists may focus their practice in particular clinical areas such as neurology, oncology, rheumatology, orthopedics, obstetrics, pediatrics, geriatrics, in the treatment of patients with cardiovascular and cardiopulmonary disorders, burns or sports injuries or in the field of ergonomics.

3142 加拿大物理治疗师的就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 要求物理治疗学专业的大学学位，而且接受过一段时间的理疗实践培训 A university degree in physiotherapy and a period of supervised practical training are required.
• 要从事该行业，必须有监管机构颁发的许可证或者登记在册 A licence or registration with a regulatory body is required to practise physiotherapy in all provinces.
• 可能要通过由理疗联盟管理委员会承办的国家物理疗法考试 Completion of the Physiotherapy National Exam, administered by the Alliance of Physiotherapy Regulatory Boards, may be required.

3142 加拿大物理治疗师的附加信息 Additional information-FLYabroad

• 随着经验的丰富，以及进一步的培训，物理治疗师可以晋升到相关管理职位，如理疗主任 Progression to management positions, such as director of physiotherapy, is possible through experience and additional training.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
