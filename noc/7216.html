7216 加拿大机械行业承包商和主管职责概述-FLYabroad

该项包括供暖、制冷、空调、磨粉厂、电梯安装行业承包商，这些承包商一般都拥有自己的公司。同时还包括负责监督和协调以下工人工作的监督员：机械装置、运输设备（机动车辆除外）机械工（见731），汽车服务技术人员（见732）和其他机械工（见733）。总之他们的任职领域很广，具体任职领域详见各组职业描述。 This unit group includes heating, refrigeration, air conditioning, millwrighting and elevator installation trade contractors who own and operate their own businesses. This group also includes supervisors who supervise and co-ordinate the activities of workers classified in unit groups within the following minor groups: Machinery and Transportation Equipment Mechanics (Except Motor Vehicle) (731), Automotive Service Technicians (732) and Other Mechanics (733). They are employed in a wide range of establishments; places of employment are indicated in the unit group descriptions.

7216 加拿大机械行业承包商和主管主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

该项中的机械行业承包商和主管应履行下列部分或全部职责： Contractors and supervisors in this unit group perform some or all of the following duties:

• 负责监督、协调并安排工人的工作，这些工人的工作主要有：飞机、轨道机车、工业机械设备、建筑等重型设备、纺织设备、印刷机器、采暖设备和热炉、变压器和其他电气设备、机动车辆、空调和制冷设备、电器和其他设备，比如摩托车、轮船舷外发动机、雪上汽车等的维修和维护 Supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of workers who repair and maintain aircraft, railway locomotives and cars, industrial machinery and equipment, construction and other heavy equipment, textile equipment, printing machines, heating equipment and furnaces, transformers and other electrical equipment, motor vehicles, air-conditioning and refrigeration equipment, electric appliances and other equipment, such as motorcycles, outboard motors and snowmobiles
• 安排工作进程确保工程按期完成，并协调好与其他部门的工作 Establish methods to meet work schedules and co-ordinate work activities with other departments
• 负责原材料的供应 Requisition materials and supplies
• 及时解决工作中遇到的问题，提出建议以提高生产率 Resolve work problems and recommend work measures to improve productivity
• 安排并负责工人培训 Train or arrange for training of workers
• 人事任职和升职 Recommend personnel actions such as hiring and promotions
• 确保工作中遵守安全工作标准 Ensure that standards for safe working conditions are observed
• 准备生产报告等 Prepare production and other reports
• 管理自己公司的运作 May manage the operations of own company
• 可能监管、协调、安排学徒、助理以及其它劳动力的工作May also supervise, co-ordinate and schedule the activities of related apprentices, helpers and labourers.

7216 加拿大机械行业承包商和主管就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 通常要求中学学历 Completion of secondary school is usually required.
• 要求具备多年的相关行业合格技工的经验 Several years of experience as a qualified tradesperson in a relevant trade are usually required.
• 通常需要具备相关行业技工证书 Journeyman/woman trade certification in a relevant trade is required.

7216 加拿大机械行业承包商和主管附加信息 Additional information-FLYabroad

• 本职业组里很少有国际贸易方面的主管人员 There is little or no intertrade mobility among the supervisors in this unit group.
