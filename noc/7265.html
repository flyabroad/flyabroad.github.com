7265 加拿大电焊工和相关行业机械设备操作员职责概述-FLYabroad

电焊工负责操作焊接设备来焊接铁和有色金属。该项还包括以前从事过制造熔接、钎焊工作的机械设备操作员。他们受雇于生产结构钢、钣金、锅炉、重型机械、飞机和船只及其它金属制品的公司，还有焊接承包商和商店，也可能自雇。 Welders operate welding equipment to weld ferrous and non-ferrous metals. This unit group also includes machine operators who operate previously set up production welding,brazing and soldering equipment. They are employed by companies that manufacture structural steel and platework, boilers, heavy machinery, aircraft and ships and other metal products,and by welding contractors and welding shops, or they may be self-employed.

7265 加拿大电焊工和相关行业机械设备操作员主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

A 电焊工和相关行业机械设备操作员应履行下列部分或全部职责：Welders perform some or all of the following duties:

• 读懂并讲解规划蓝图或焊接工艺规范 Read and interpret blueprints or welding process specifications
• 操作人工或半自动焊接设备熔解金属，如气体钨电弧焊（又称氩弧焊）、气体金属电弧焊（又称气体保护焊）、药芯焊丝电弧焊（又称气体保护焊）、等离子电弧焊(又称电浆弧焊)、手工电弧焊（SMAW）、氧气乙炔焰焊接（OAW）、电阻焊接和潜弧焊（SAW） Operate manual or semi-automatic welding equipment to fuse metal segments using processes such as gas tungsten arc welding (GTAW), gas metal arc welding (GMAW), flux-cored arc welding (FCAW), plasma arc welding (PAW), shielded metal arc welding (SMAW), oxy-acetylene welding (OAW), resistance welding and submerged arc welding (SAW)
• 操作人工或半自动气割设备 Operate manual or semi-automatic flame-cutting equipment
• 操作钎焊及低温焊接设备Operate brazing and soldering equipment
• 操作金属成型机，如弯板机、剪床和其它金属拉直或弯曲机 Operate metal shaping machines such as brakes, shears and other metal straightening and bending machines
• 焊接额外层以修复磨损金属制品的零部件 Repair worn parts of metal products by welding on extra layers.
• 焊工可能专门从事某一类行业焊接工作，如传统制造业、航空航天精密焊接、压力容器焊接、管道施工焊接、焊接结构施工或者机器和设备的维修焊接 Welders may specialize in certain types of welding such as custom fabrication, ship building and repair, aerospace precision welding, pressure vessel welding, pipeline construction welding, structural construction welding, or machinery and equipment repair welding.

B 焊接、钎焊和低温焊接操作员应履行下列部分或全部职责： Welding, brazing and soldering machine operators perform some or all of the following duties:

• 操作预设的焊接机，如点焊机、接缝电阻焊机及天然气弧焊机等，来制造或修复金属零件 Operate previously set up welding machines such as spot, butt and seam resistance or gas and arc welding machines to fabricate or repair metal parts
• 操作预设的钎焊或低温焊接机器来焊接金属零件，或用锡来填补漏洞、缺口和金属接缝 Operate previously set up brazing or soldering machines to bond metal parts or to fill holes, indentations and seams of metal articles with solder
• 启动、关闭、调整和监控自动焊接生产线 Start up, shut down, adjust and monitor robotic welding production line
• 协助维护和修理焊接、钎焊和低温焊接设备 Assist with the maintenance and repair of welding, brazing and soldering equipment
• 可能负责根据工作规范来调整焊接头和模具 May adjust welding heads and tooling according to work specifications.

7265 加拿大电焊工和相关行业机械设备操作员就业条件 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

A 电焊工 Welders

• 通常要求中学文化程度 Completion of secondary school is usually required.
• 完成三年的学徒训练或具有相关专业三年以上的工作经验并且完成了焊接专业的大学或工业课程的学习，才有资格获得技工证书 Completion of a three-year apprenticeship program or A combination of over three years of work experience in the trade and some college or industry courses in welding is usually required to be eligible for trade certification.
• 在阿尔伯特省可以且必须获得贸技工证书，但在纽芬兰和拉布拉多省、爱德华王子岛、新斯科舍、新布伦瑞克省、马尼托巴、萨斯克彻温省、不列颠哥伦比亚省、努纳武特、西北和育空地区则是自愿的 Trade certification is compulsory in Alberta and available, but voluntary, in Newfoundland and Labrador, Prince Edward Island, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, British Columbia, Nunavut, the Northwest Territories and the Yukon.
• 合格的焊工能获得全国通用的省级技工证书 Interprovincial trade certification (Red Seal) is also available to qualified welders.

B 焊接、钎焊、低温焊接机器操作员 Welding, brazing and soldering machine operators

• 需要一些中学教育经历 Some secondary school education is required.
• 通常需要多个月的在职培训经历 Several months of on-the-job training are usually provided.
• 需要有操作员助理的相关经验 Experience as a machine operator helper may be required.
• 需要具有操作自动生产线的经验 Experience with robotics may be required.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
