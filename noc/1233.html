1233 加拿大保险理算员及理赔审核员 Insurance Adjusters and Claims Examiners-FLYabroad

1233 加拿大保险理算员及理赔审核员职责概述-FLYabroad

保险理算员主要负责调查保险索赔，并确定在保单承担范围内的损失数额。他们受雇于保险公司的理赔部门，或者担任独立的理赔员。保险理赔审核员负责核查理算员调查研究的索赔数额并执行赔偿支付。他们受雇于保险公司的总公司或者分公司。 Insurance adjusters investigate insurance claims and determine the amount of loss or damages covered by insurance policies. They are employed in claims departments of insurance companies or as independent adjusters. Insurance claims examiners examine claims investigated by insurance adjusters and authorize payments. They are employed at head offices or branches of insurance companies.

1233 加拿大保险理算员及理赔审核员主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

A 保险理算员主要履行以下部分或者全部职责： Insurance adjusters perform some or all of the following duties:

• 调查保险索赔的情况以确定索赔的有效性 Investigate circumstances surrounding insurance claims to determine validity of claim
• 检查汽车、住宅及其它财产的损失 Inspect automobile, home or other property damage
• 发表声明，与索赔人、事故目击者、医生和其它有关人员协商并彻查报告和记录 Take statements and consult with claimants, accident witnesses, doctors and other relevant individuals and examine records or reports
• 确定保单范围内的损失 Determine amount of loss or damages covered by insurance policies
• 协商解决索赔 Negotiate settlement of claims
• 准备赔偿报告 Prepare adjustment reports.

B 保险理赔审核员主要履行以下部分或者全部职责： Insurance claims examiners perform some or all of the following duties:

• 评审、检查、计算并执行保险理算员调查研究的理赔数额 Review, examine, calculate and authorize insurance claims investigated by insurance adjusters
• 核查理算员的报告以及相似的保险索赔先例，以确定保险责任范围 Examine adjusters' reports and similar insurance claims or precedents to determine extent of insurance coverage
• 确保理赔的合理性，并制定符合公司规定的理赔诉讼手续 Ensure claims are valid and settlements are made according to company practices and procedures
• 向律师、医生以及其它相关人员咨询请教，商讨理赔事宜 Consult lawyers, doctors or other relevant individuals to discuss insurance claims
• 为汽车、火灾、人身、伤残、牙齿以及其它保险理赔提供证据 Approve automobile, fire, life, disability, dental or other insurance claims.

1233 加拿大保险理算员及理赔审核员就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 要求有高中学学历 Completion of secondary school is required.
• 要求学士学位、大学文凭或者大专教育，或者有多年理赔部门文员或一般保险工作的工作经验 A bachelor's degree, college diploma or some post-secondary education or Several years of experience as a clerk in the claims department or other general insurance experience are required.
• 要求有多年在职培训的经历并且完成保险业课程和培训计划 Several years of on-the-job training and completion of insurance industry courses and training programs are required.
• 独立的理算员需要由省级保险机关颁或雇佣单位颁发省级执照 Independent adjusters require a provincial licence issued by the Superintendent of Insurance in the province or territory of employment.

1233 加拿大保险理算员及理赔审核员附加信息 Additional information-FLYabroad

• 完成加拿大保险协会或者省级机构的教育规划后，保险理算员和理赔审核员将得到职业认可，并成为受特许的保险专业人员（CIP），接受了附加大学课程教育的称为资深保险专业人员 Completion of educational programs through the Insurance Institute of Canada or its provincial counterparts entitles insurance adjusters and claims examiners to professional recognition as a Chartered Insurance Professional (CIP), and with additional university courses, as a Fellow Chartered Insurance Professional (FCIP).

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民的紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
