3131 加拿大药剂师的职责概述-FLYabroad

社区药剂师和医院药剂师负责调制并分发规定药品，并为客户和医疗服务人员提供咨询服务。他们受雇于社区或医院药房，或者自雇。工业药剂师参与药剂产品的研究、开发、推广和生产。他们受雇于制药厂、政府机构或药品代理处。Community pharmacists and hospital pharmacists compound and dispense prescribed pharmaceuticals and provide consultative services to both clients and health care providers. They are employed in community and hospital pharmacies, or they may be self-employed. Industrial pharmacists participate in the research, development, promotion and manufacture of pharmaceutical products. They are employed in pharmaceutical companies and government departments and agencies.

3131 加拿大药剂师的主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

A 社区药剂师和医院药剂师应履行下列部分或全部职责： Community pharmacists and hospital pharmacists perform some or all of the following duties:

• 检查处方剂量 Check prescriptions for proper dosage
• 通过计算和测定的数据以及需要的药物和其他成分，通过容器来混合正确数量的处方药品 Compound prescribed pharmaceutical products by calculating, measuring and mixing the quantities of drugs and other ingredients required and filling appropriate containers with correct quantity
• 为客户或者其他医疗保健专业人士分配规定的药品，并就适应症、禁忌，不良反应和药物的相互作用和用药剂量为他们提供建议 Dispense prescribed pharmaceuticals to customers or to other health care professionals and advise them on indications, contra-indications, adverse effects, drug interactions and dosage
• 为客户维护药剂文档，包括已经注册的药物、麻醉药和受管制的药物 Maintain medication profiles of customers including registry of poisons and narcotic and controlled drugs
• 确保疫苗、血清、生物制剂和其他药品和药物的适当的准备、封装、分发和储存 Ensure proper preparation, packaging, distribution and storage of vaccines, serums, biologicals and other drugs and pharmaceuticals
• 负责药物的订购和存货的维护 Order and maintain stock of pharmaceutical supplies
• 在非处方药的选择和使用上给客户提供建议 Advise customers on selection and use of non-prescription medication
• 可能管理和协调其他药剂师、药房助理、助理药剂师和其他工作人员的工作 May supervise and co-ordinate the activities of other pharmacists, pharmacy assistants, pharmacy technicians and other staff.

B 工业药剂师应履行下列部分或全部职责： Industrial pharmacists perform some or all of the following duties:

• 参与新药品开发的基础研究 Participate in basic research for the development of new drugs
• 对药品研究院研制的新药品做明确的说明 Formulate new drug products developed by medical researchers
• 测试新药品的稳定性，并确定他们的吸收和排除的方式 Test new drug products for stability and to determine their absorption and elimination patterns
• 协调新药品的临床研究 Co-ordinate clinical investigations of new drugs
• 在生产过程中监控新药品的产品质量，确保他们符合药效、纯度、均匀性、稳定性和安全性标准 Control the quality of drug products during production to ensure that they meet standards of potency, purity, uniformity, stability and safety
• 设计关于特殊药品的使用、性能及风险的信息资料 Develop information materials concerning the uses, properties and risks of particular drugs
• 评估新药品的产品标签、包装和宣传事宜 Evaluate labelling, packaging and advertising of drug products
• 向专业保健人员推广医药产品 Promote pharmaceutical products to health professionals.

3131 加拿大药剂师就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 需要有药剂学学士学位 A bachelor of science degree in pharmacy is required.
• 需要有在药剂师监督指导下的实习训练经历 Pharmacists also require practical training under the supervision of a pharmacist.
• 所有省级、地方的社区药剂师和医院药剂师，都需要有相关的许可证 Licensure is required in all provinces and territories for community and hospital pharmacists.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民的紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
