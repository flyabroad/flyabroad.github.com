1225 采购代理及相关人员 Purchasing Agents and Officers

1225 采购代理人员主要负责购买一般及专用设备，原材料或商业服务用于自用或其公司的再加工使用，1225 职业受雇于各公共，私人部门。
Purchasing agents and officers purchase general and specialized equipment, materials and business services for use or for further processing by their establishment. They are employed throughout the public and private sectors.

合同管理官员 contract management officer

1225 主要职责 Main duties

• 购买一般或特殊设备，为再加工提供的原料或商业服务。
Purchase general and specialized equipment, materials or business services for use or for further processing by their establishment

• 评估机构需要并且开发设备，材料及供应品购买的规范。
Assess requirements of an establishment and develop specifications for equipment, materials and supplies to be purchased

• 招标、 与供应商协商并查看报价。
Invite tenders, consult with suppliers and review quotations

• 确定或对合约条款及条件进行谈判，签订供应合同或推荐合同签订。
Determine or negotiate contract terms and conditions, award supplier contracts or recommend contract awards

• 建立交货日程表，监控进度，与客户，供应商联系以解决问题。
Establish delivery schedules, monitor progress and contact clients and suppliers to resolve problems

• 雇佣，培训，监督采购员。
May hire, train or supervise purchasing clerks.

• 采购代理，主管可能专门负责特殊材料的采购，如木材，办公设备的商业服务。
Purchasing agents and officers may specialize in the purchase of particular materials or business services such as furniture or office equipment.

1225 雇佣要求 Employment requirements

• 经营管理，商业或经济学等相关大学，大专学历。A bachelor's degree or college diploma in business administration, commerce or economics is usually required.
• 购买专业材料或提供专业商业服务采购代理官员要求相关专业大学或大专学历。比如，工业产品采购需要有工程类大学或大专学历。Purchasing agents and officers purchasing specialized materials or business services may require a related university degree or college diploma. For example, a bachelor's degree or college diploma in engineering may be required for purchasers of industrial products.
• 有些可能需要有采购管理协会颁发的采购证书。A certificate in purchasing from the Purchasing Management Association of Canada may be required.
• 行政办事员要求有作为采购员的经验。Previous experience as a purchasing clerk or as an administrative clerk may be required.

1225 其他信息 Additional information

• 有工作经验可能会升迁到管理职位。Progression to managerial positions is possible with experience.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民的紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
