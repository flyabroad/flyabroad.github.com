1113 有价证券代理，投资商和经纪人 （1113 Securities Agents, Investment Dealers and Brokers）

1113 有价证券代理，投资商为证券投资者、退休基金经理，银行，信贷公司，保险公司及其他机构购买和出售股票，债券，财政账单，公共基金，和其他证券。经纪人代表投资商在证券交易所买卖股票，商品期货，外币，和其他证券。他们一般供职于保险行业，股票经纪人公司，股票和商品期货和证券行业其他机构。
Securities agents and investment dealers buy and sell stocks, bonds, treasury bills, mutual funds and other securities for individual investors, pension fund managers, banks, trust companies, insurance firms and other establishments. Brokers buy and sell stocks, bonds, commodity futures, foreign currencies and other securities at stock exchanges on behalf of investment dealers. They are employed by investment companies, stock brokerage firms, stock and commodity exchanges and other establishments in the securities industry.

1113 有价证券代理，投资商和经纪人主要职责（Main duties）

1113 有价证券代理，投资商主要职责（Securities agents and investment dealers perform some or all of the following duties）

通过准备投资策略帮助客户达成他们的财政目地，给出关于各种类投资的建议和信息来指导他们完成证券投资组合。
Develop long-term relationships with their clients by preparing investment strategies to help them achieve their financial goals, offering advice and information on various investments to guide them in managing their portfolios

回顾财政期刊，股市债券报告，商业出版和其他材料，并在金融业内发展局域网来搜集相关信息并识别潜能的客户投资。
Review financial periodicals, stock and bond reports, business publications and other material, and develop a network within the financial industry to gather relevant information and identify potential investments for clients

通过买卖股票，债券，国库券，公共基金和其他证券为独立投资者、退休基金经理，银行，信贷公司，保险公司等其他公司机构实施投资指令。
Carry out investment orders by buying and selling stocks, bonds, treasury bills, mutual funds and other securities for individual investors, pension fund managers or companies such as banks, trust companies and insurance firms

监督客户的投资证券组合，并确保投资交是根据产业规程正常实施。
Monitor clients' investment portfolios and ensure that investment transactions are carried out according to industry regulations.

1113 经纪人主要职责：（Brokers perform some or all of the following duties）

代表投资商在股票交易所买卖股票，债券，商业期货，外汇和其他证券
Buy and sell stocks, bonds, commodity futures, foreign currencies and other securities at stock exchanges on behalf of investment dealers

通过回顾投资信息和在证券交易所监控市场状况和联系其他投资公司的贸易部门，退休金基金经理，和金融分析师来发展交易战略。
Develop trading strategies by reviewing investment information and monitoring market conditions from the exchange floor and through contact with trading departments of other investment firms, pension fund managers and financial analysts

竞标、要约买卖证券和完成其他关于交换有价证券的细节
Make bids and offers to buy and sell securities and complete details of sales on exchange trade tickets

为回顾证券投资组合状态做准备
Prepare reviews of portfolios' positions.

1113 有价证券代理，投资商和经纪人雇佣条件Employment requirements

要求经济和商业或其他学科的本科及研究生学位 A bachelor's or master's degree in economics, business or other discipline is usually required.
销售代表和证券经纪行业的投资需要完成销售培训计划和加拿大证券课程，考试注册代表手册完成由加拿大证券学院提供。Securities sales representatives and brokers require completion of industry investment and sales training programs and completion of the Canadian Securities course and the Registered Representative Manual Exam offered by the Canadian Securities Institute.
专门课程同样适用于证券销售代表，希望经营某特殊投资产品比如证券选项和期货合同的经纪人 Specialized courses are also available for securities sales representatives and brokers who want to trade in specific investment products such as securities options and futures contracts.
要求证券代理，投资商和经纪人有被雇佣所在省的省证券委员会的许可证 Licensure by the provincial securities commission in the province of employment is required for securities agents, investment dealers and brokers.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
