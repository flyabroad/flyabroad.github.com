2143 加拿大采矿工程师 Mining Engineers

属于加拿大联邦技术移民 227 新政后的38 大类移民紧缺职业（2008.11.28）。

加拿大采矿工程师职责概述

采矿工程师规划，设计，组织和监管矿山，矿山设施，系统和设备的发展计划；金属或非金属矿物提炼和地下或矿山表层采矿工作的准备和监管。
Mining engineers plan, design, organize and supervise the development of mines, mine facilities, systems and equipment; and prepare and supervise the extraction of metallic or non-metallic minerals and ores from underground or surface mines

采矿工程师们受雇于矿业公司，工程咨询公司，制造商，政府，教育和研究机构。
Mining engineers are employed by mining companies, consulting engineering companies, manufacturers, government and in educational and research institutions

加拿大采矿工程师主要职责 Main duties

采矿工程师履行下列部分或全部职责
Mining engineers perform some or all of the following duties

进行矿石，煤炭矿产或储藏的初步调查和研究，以评估潜在采矿业务的经济和环境可行性
Conduct preliminary surveys and studies of ore, mineral or coal deposits to assess the economic and environmental feasibility of potential mining operations

确定适当的手段安全有效地挖掘矿藏
Determine the appropriate means of safely and efficiently mining deposits

为采矿，建筑或拆毁工作确定并建议适当的爆破方法
Determine and advise on appropriate drilling and blasting methods for mining, construction or demolition

竖井，通风系统，矿井服务，运输系统和支撑结构的设计
Design shafts, ventilation systems, mine services, haulage systems and supporting structures

设计，制定和实施计算机应用程序，用于矿井设计，矿山模型，绘图或监测矿井条件等方面
Design, develop and implement computer applications such as for mine design, mine modelling, mapping or for monitoring mine conditions

与其他工程专家一起规划，设计或选择采矿设备及机械，矿石标准及设备
Plan and design or select mining equipment and machinery and mineral treatment machinery and equipment in collaboration with other engineering specialists

计划，组织和监管矿山和矿山结构的开拓以及矿山的经营和维护
Plan, organize and supervise the development of mines and mine structures and the operation and maintenance of mines

编写业务和项目概算，时间安排和报告
Prepare operations and project estimates, schedules and reports

实施和协调矿山安全计划
Implement and co-ordinate mine safety programs

监管和统筹技术人员，技术专家，调查人员及其他工程师和科学家的工作
Supervise and co-ordinate the work of technicians, technologists, survey personnel, and other engineers and scientists.

加拿大采矿工程师职业要求及移民条件 Employment requirements

要求具备采矿工程学学士学位或者经过了相关工程学的培训。
A bachelor's degree in mining engineering or in a related engineering discipline is required

某些相关工程学科可能需要硕士或博士学位。
A master's degree or doctorate in a related engineering discipline may be required

工程制图和报告的审核批准或作为一名专业工程师须获得省或地区专业工程师协会颁发的许可证。
Licensing by a provincial or territorial association of professional engineers is required to approve engineering drawings and reports and to practise as a Professional Engineer (P.Eng.)

具备注册资格的工程师需通过国家认可的专业教育，而且在工程监管方面有三到四年的工作经验，且通过了专业的实践考试。
Engineers are eligible for registration following graduation from an accredited educational program, and after three or four years of supervised work experience in engineering and passing a professional practice examination

监事会督导及其他高级职位需要具备多年工作经验。
Supervisory and senior positions in this unit group require experience

附加信息Additional information

多学科的工作环境使工程师们有机会接触到多领域的知识，这就使得工程师可以从事其他相关领域的工作，比如科学，工程，销售，营销或管理
Engineers often work in a multidisciplinary environment and acquire knowledge and skills through work experience that may allow them to practise in associated areas of science, engineering, sales, marketing or management

矿物工程师与地质学家，地质工程师，冶金工程师，及其它相关工程师或科学家的工作密切相连，所以某些专业领域之间存在一定的流动性
Mining engineers work closely with geologists, geological engineers, metallurgical engineers, and other engineers and scientists, and mobility is possible between some fields of specialization

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
