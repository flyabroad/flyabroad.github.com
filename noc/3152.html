3152 加拿大注册护士 Registered Nurses-FLYabroad

3152 加拿大注册护士职责概述-FLYabroad

该项中包括注册护士、护师、注册精神科护士和那些结束护理培训等待注册的毕业生们（经过护士学校训练的护士）。他们给病人提供直接的护理服务、制定健康教育计划并对与护理业务相关的问题提供建议。他们的就业范围很广，比如像：医院、疗养院、延长护理服务机构、康复中心、医生办公室、诊所、社区机构、公司和私人家庭或者自雇。 This unit group includes registered nurses, nurse practitioners, registered psychiatric nurses and graduates of a nursing program who are awaiting registration (graduate nurses). They provide direct nursing care to patients, deliver health education programs and provide consultative services regarding issues relevant to the practice of nursing. They are employed in a variety of settings including hospitals, nursing homes, extended care facilities, rehabilitation centres, doctors' offices, clinics, community agencies, companies and private homes, or they may be self-employed.

3152 加拿大注册护士主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

一般责任注册护士应履行下列部分或全部职责： General duty registered nurses perform some or all of the following duties:

• 根据病人的情况选择适当的护理措施 Assess patients to identify appropriate nursing interventions
• 与跨学科的医疗队合作，通过与病人及其家属的磋商来企划、实施、统筹、评估病人的护理情况 Collaborate with members of an interdisciplinary health team to plan, implement, co-ordinate and evaluate patient care in consultation with patients and their families
• 根据医师的指示或者现有的医疗政策和协议，来管理药物治疗方案 Administer medications and treatments as prescribed by a physician or according to established policies and protocols
• 监控、评估、讨论、记录并报告病人的症状以及病情的变化 Monitor, assess, address, document and report symptoms and changes in patients' conditions
• 操作或监控医疗器械和设备 Operate or monitor medical apparatus or equipment
• 协助做手术及其他的医疗程序 Assist in surgery and other medical procedures
• 可能负责管理持证护士和其他护理人员 May supervise licensed practical nurses and other nursing staff
• 可能负责为入院病人制定和实施出院计划 May develop and implement discharge planning process on admission of patients
• 或许会跟其他医护人员一起，向病人及其家属传授一些保健方面的知识并提供相应的建议 May teach and counsel patients and their families on health-related issues in collaboration with other health care providers.
• 注册护士可能专门负责这些领域，如：外科、妇产科护理、精神科护理、重症监护、儿科、老年科、社区保健、职业保健、急救护理或肿瘤科等 Registered nurses may specialize in areas such as surgery, obstetrics care, psychiatric care, critical care, pediatrics, geriatrics, community health, occupational health, emergency care, rehabilitation or oncology.
• 跟注册护士相比，护师可以独立提供更广范围的医疗服务 Nurse practitioners are independent care providers with a broader scope of practice relative to registered nurses.
• 职业保健护士负责制定和实施员工的保健教育计划并为私营企业和行业提供注册护理服务 Occupational health nurses develop and implement employee health education programs and provide registered nursing care in private businesses and industry.
• 社区保健护士负责为公共卫生单位提供保健教育和注册护理服务，并通过家访的方式，处理复杂的家庭护理、参与社区护理需求评估和程序制定、进行疾病筛查和提供免疫接种 Community health nurses provide health education and registered nursing care in public health units and through home visits, manage complex home care cases, participate in community needs assessment and program development, conduct disease screening and deliver immunization programs.
• 精神科护士负责为精神病医院、心里健康诊所、长期护理机构和社区的病人，提供护理、援助式辅导和制定生活技能规划 Psychiatric nurses provide nursing care, supportive counselling and life skills programming to patients in psychiatric hospitals, mental health clinics, long-term care facilities and community-based settings.
• 护理顾问负责就有关护理职业和护理程序的问题，为机构、社团和卫生保健组织提供咨询服务 Nursing consultants provide consultative services to institutes, associations and health care organizations regarding issues and concerns relevant to the nursing profession and nursing practice.
• 从事护理研究的护理研究员，一般是自雇或者受雇于医院、公共或私人机构以及政府 Nursing researchers engaged in research activities related to nursing are self-employed or are employed by hospitals, public and private organizations and governments.
• 临床护士负责为由特定的医疗机构转交来的特定病人群体，提供基于研究型护理的领导、建议和咨询 Clinical nurses provide leadership, advice and counsel on the provision of research-based care for specific patient groups within the care of particular health care organizations.

3152 加拿大注册护士就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

A 对注册护士要求 Registered nurses

• 必须完成大学、学院或者其他经认可的学校护理专业的学习 Completion of a university, college or other approved registered nursing program is required.
• 若想深入研究护理专业的某一领域，必须接受过护理领域专门的培训或者拥有丰富的临床经验 Additional academic training or experience is required to specialize in a specific area of nursing.
• 临床护理专家、临床护士、护理顾问以及护理研究人员一般要求具有硕士或博士学位 A master's or doctoral degree in nursing is usually required for clinical nurse specialists, clinical nurses, nursing consultants and nursing researchers.
• 所有的省份和地区普遍要求从业人员必须在相关管理机构进行过护士注册 Registration with a regulatory body is required in all provinces and territories.

B 对护师的要求 Nurse practitioners

• 要求具有护理专业硕士学位，或者接受过护理教育，或者有高级护师教育经历 A master's degree in nursing, or a nursing program or other advanced nurse practitioner diploma program is required.
• 所有的省份和地区普遍要求从业人员必须在相关管理机构进行过护士注册 Registration with a regulatory body is required in all provinces and territories
• 在安大略省，要求注册护士必须成功通过扩展班注册考试 In Ontario, successful completion of the Extended Class Registration Examination (ECRE) is required for registration as Registered Nurse in the Extended Class RN(EC).

C 对注册精神科护士的要求 Registered psychiatric nurses

• 必须完成了大学或者学院级的精神科护理专业的学习 Completion of a university or college registered psychiatric nursing program is required.
• 在马尼托巴省、萨斯克彻温省、艾伯塔省和不列颠哥伦比亚省，这些地区要求护士必须在相关机构进行了注册 Registration with a regulatory body is required in Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta and British Columbia.

3152 加拿大注册护士附加信息 Additional information-FLYabroad

• 在马尼托巴省、萨斯克彻温省、艾伯塔省和不列颠哥伦比亚省，要求注册精神科护士必须是训练有素的。而在其他省份和地区，注册护士不需要其他的注册就可以以精神科护士的身份工作 Nurses trained exclusively as registered psychiatric nurses (RPN) are regulated in Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta and British Columbia. In all other provinces and territories, registered nurses (RN) may work as psychiatric nurses without separate registration.Registered nurses may progress to supervisory and managerial positions with experience.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。

新政后紧缺职业移民同样要求达到及格分，技术移民算分很多人关心的问题：雅思需要考几个 7 （现在应该说，考几个 high）。
