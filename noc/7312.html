7312 加拿大重型机械设备技工职责概述-FLYabroad

重型机械设备技工负责维修、排查、调整、检修和维护移动重型设备，这些设备主要用于建筑、交通、林矿业、石油天然气业、物料搬运、环境美化、场地清理、农业以及类似的相关行业。他们受雇于拥有和经营重型设备的公司、重型设备经销商、租赁和服务机构、铁路运输企业和城市轨道交通系统。 Heavy-duty equipment mechanics repair, troubleshoot, adjust, overhaul and maintain mobile heavy-duty equipment used in construction, transportation, forestry, mining, oil and gas, material handling, landscaping, land clearing, farming and similar activities. They are employed by companies which own and operate heavy equipment, and by heavy equipment dealers,rental and service establishments, and railway transport companies and urban transit systems.

7312 加拿大重型机械设备技工主要职责 Main duties-FLYabroad

重型机械设备技工应履行下列部分或全部职责： Heavy-duty equipment mechanics perform some or all of the following duties:

• 检查推土机、起重机、平土机和其它重型建筑、农业、伐木和采矿设备，以确保其能正常运作，并对设备进行故障检测 Check bulldozers, cranes, graders and other heavy construction, agricultural, logging and mining equipment for proper performance and inspect equipment to detect faults and malfunctions
• 使用电脑和其它诊断设备诊断机器故障以确定修复需要的时间 Diagnose faults or malfunctions using computerized and other testing equipment to determine extent of repair required
• 利用手动或电动工具调整设备、修理或更换磨损的零部件甚至是系统 Adjust equipment and repair or replace defective parts, components or systems, using hand and power tools
• 测试修好的设备的性能，确保产品恢复原来的功能并和生产厂商的说明一致 Test repaired equipment for proper performance and to ensure that work meets manufacturers' specifications
• 给机器清洁、涂润滑油或做一些其它日常性的维护工作 Clean, lubricate and perform other routine maintenance work on equipment
• 操作服务附件和工作工具，如收割和耕作设备、铲（推土机等的)、犁、绞车和侧臂吊管机 Service attachments and working tools such as harvesting and tillage equipment, blades, ploughs, winches and side booms
• 可能负责重型卡车的修理 May perform repair work on heavy trucks
• 可能负责安装组件和调整新型农用设备 May attach components and adjust new farm equipment.
• 重型和农业机械设备技工可能是这方面的专家，如联合或履带车辆、发动机翻修、动力变换输送、燃油喷射、液压或电子学等 Heavy-duty and farm equipment mechanics may specialize in specific types of machinery such as combines or tracked vehicles, or in engine overhaul, power shift transmissions, fuel injection, hydraulics or electronics.

7312 加拿大重型机械设备技工就业要求 Employment requirements-FLYabroad

• 通常要求中学文化程度 Completion of secondary school is usually required.
• 有三到五年的学徒训练，或者有四年以上技工工作经验以及重型机械修理专业的高中，大学或工业课程的学习，才有资格获得技工证书 Completion of a three- to five-year apprenticeship program or A combination of over four years of work experience in the trade and some high school, college or industry courses in heavy equipment repair is usually required to be eligible for trade certification.
• 在魁北克省和阿尔伯塔省，可以且必须获得重型机械设备技工证书，但在其他省份和地区则是自愿的 Heavy-duty equipment mechanic trade certification is compulsory in Quebec and Alberta and available, but voluntary, in all other provinces and the territories.
• 在纽芬兰和拉布拉多省、爱德华王子岛、新斯科舍、新布伦瑞克省、安大略省、马尼托巴、萨斯克彻温省、阿尔伯塔省和不列颠哥伦比亚省、可以获得农用机械设备技工证书，但是并非必须，都是自愿的 Farm equipment mechanic trade certification is available, but voluntary, in Newfoundland and Labrador, Prince Edward Island, Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Ontario, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta and British Columbia.
• 合格的重型机械设备技术人员和农业机械设备技工可以获得全国通用的省级技工证书 Interprovincial trade certification (Red Seal) is also available to qualified heavy-duty equipment technicians and farm equipment mechanics.

7312 加拿大重型机械设备技工附加信息 Additional information-FLYabroad

• 具备了一定经验之后有机会晋升为主管 Progression to supervisory positions is possible with experience.
• 通用的技工证书可以在省级之间流动使用 Red Seal trade certification allows for interprovincial mobility.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
