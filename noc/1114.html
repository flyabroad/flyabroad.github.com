NOC 1114 其他财政工作者 Other Financial Officers

NOC 1114 单元组包括财务类专业性职业，不另分类为金融顾问，财政监察员，和检查官，财政调查员，财政承销商，抵押借款经纪人和其他信贷办公人员。他们一般都受雇于银行，信贷公司，投资公司和政府，或者他们私营。
This unit group includes professional occupations in finance not elsewhere classified such as financial planners, financial examiners and inspectors, financial investigators, financial underwriters, mortgage brokers and trust officers. They are employed by banks, trust companies, investment firms and governments, or they may be self-employed.

NOC 1114 主要职责 Main duties

财政计划员为客户发展财政计划，将覆盖如下方面：现金管理，资产，保险责任范围，投资，投宿和产业规划，赋税和法律事务；分析客户的财务报告，制定金融目标和战略；通过建议客户完成财政计划来帮助他们完成其目标；同时安排金融产品和有牌照的投资，同时监控证券投资组合来确保它的质量和收益性；并来帮助拓展业务，吸引新客户。
Financial planners develop personal financial plans for clients covering cash management, finances, insurance coverage, investments, retirement and estate planning, taxes and legal matters; analyze clients' financial records, set goals and develop a financial strategy; advise clients on implementing the financial plan to help them achieve their goals; may also arrange for the sale of financial products and investments depending on the licence held, and monitor the portfolio to ensure its quality and profitability; and help to expand business and attract new clients.

金融考官和检查员审查和研究银行，信托投资公司，信用合作社，真正的房地产公司，保险公司，抚恤基金，证券经纪人和其他金融服务和机构进而确保符合政府的立法和规程。
Financial examiners and inspectors review and examine banks, trust companies, credit unions, caisses populaires, credit companies, real estate companies, insurance companies, pension funds, securities brokers and other financial services and institutions to ensure compliance with governing legislation and regulations.

财政调查者审查提供证券和期货或类似业务的法人和公司可能存在的缺乏职业道德的行为或者不合证券和期货法的地方
Financial investigators investigate possible unethical conduct or breaches of securities or commodity futures laws by persons or companies that trade in securities and commodity futures or that provide related financial services.

财务承销商包销股票和债券，与企业和政府进行谈判以确定新已发行的证券的类型和条款并准备提供招股章程
Financial underwriters underwrite new issues of stocks and bonds, negotiate with corporations and governments to determine the type and terms of new securities issued and prepare offering prospectuses.

信贷管理员管理房地产，个人，慈善机构，企业和其他类型的直接信贷帐号投资，接受并记录投资收入并支付信贷基金
Trust officers administer estate, personal, charitable, corporate and other types of trusts, direct trust account investments, receive and record investment income and disburse trust funds.

按揭经纪人与客户会面，以获取有关收入，负债和资产的信息，以财产的类型，价格，和条件作抵押，并代表客户和债权人或者借贷机构协商
Mortgage brokers meet with clients to obtain information about income, liabilities and assets, and type, price and condition of property to be mortgaged, and negotiate mortgage loans with lenders or lending institutions on behalf of clients.

NOC 1114 雇佣条件 Employment requirements

通常需要工商管理，商业，经济或其他相关专业的本科学士学历 A bachelor's degree in business administration, commerce, economics or a related field is usually required.
可能需要通过金融机构和组织如加拿大证券专业学会，加拿大银行家协会，特许金融分析师研究所，投资基金协会，加拿大财务规划学院和加拿大信托公司，提供的各种培训计划和课程 Various training programs and courses are offered by financial institutes and organizations, such as the Canadian Securities Institute, Institute of Canadian Bankers, Institute of Chartered Financial Analysts, Investment Funds Institute, Canadian Institute of Financial Planning, and Trust Companies Institute of Canada, and may be required by employers.
可能要求有由加拿大理财规划师标准委员会颁发的个人理财规划师的认定。The designation Personal Financial Planner, awarded by the Financial Planners Standards Council of Canada, may be required.
出售比如养老金，金储蓄计划和生命保险等一些有政府规定的金融产品和投资的金融顾问要求有适当的政府机构的授权 Financial planners who sell regulated financial products and investments, such as annuities, RRSPs and life insurance, are required to be licensed by the appropriate governing body.
金融考官和检查员可能需要一个公认的会计指定 Financial examiners and inspectors may require a recognized accounting designation.
按揭经纪人需要在魁北克房地产执照，纽芬兰和拉布拉多，新斯科舍省，安大略省，曼尼托巴，萨斯喀彻温省，阿尔伯塔省，不列颠哥伦比亚省的的按揭经纪人执照 Mortgage brokers require a real estate licence in Quebec and a mortgage broker licence in Newfoundland and Labrador, Nova Scotia, Ontario, Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta and British Columbia.

NOC 1114 其他信息 Additional information

财务管理职位可能要求有相关工作经验 Progression to financial management positions is possible with experience.
尽管这些职位需要高水平的训练和水平，但是仍要求供职者必须不断更新自己的技能并追求其专业发展 While these kinds of positions require a high degree of training and qualifications, persons who occupy them must continually update their skills and pursue professional development.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
