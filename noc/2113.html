2113 地理学家、地球物理学家 Geologists, Geochemists and Geophysicists

属于加拿大联邦技术移民 227 新政后的38 大类移民紧缺职业（2008.11.28）。

加拿大地理学家、地球物理学家职责概述

地质学家、地球化学工作者和地球物理学者主要工作：探索研究地球结构、地球组成及地球运动过程，定位并确认碳氢化合物、矿藏和地下水资源位置，计划和执行碳氢化合物和矿物开采计划，评估并减轻发展及污水治理工程对环境带来的影响。（Geologists, geochemists and geophysicists conduct programs of exploration and research to extend knowledge of the structure, composition and processes of the earth, to locate and identify hydrocarbon, mineral and groundwater resources, to plan and implement programs of hydrocarbon and mineral extraction and to assess and mitigate the effects of development and waste disposal projects on the environment.）

地质学家、地球化学工作者和地球物理学者受雇于石油矿业公司，地质学咨询公司，地球物理学及工程公司，政府及教育机构，也可以自谋职业。（They are employed by petroleum and mining companies, consulting geology, geophysics and engineering firms and by governments and educational institutions or they may be self-employed）

加拿大地理学家、地球物理学家职业要求 Employment requirements

地质学，地球化学，地球物理学或相关学科大学学位。（A university degree in geology, geochemistry, geophysics or a related discipline is required）

地球物理学者需要地球物理学，物理，数学或工程学方面的硕士或博士学位。（A master's or doctoral degree in geophysics, physics, mathematics or engineering may be required for employment as a geophysicist）

如果在纽芬兰和拉布拉多，艾伯塔，不列颠哥伦比亚，努纳武特地区和西北地区实际工作需要在相应省份的专业工程师或地质、地球物理学家协会注册。（Registration as a professional geologist or professional geophysicist by a provincial or territorial association of professional engineers or geologists and geophysicists is often required for employment and to practice in Newfoundland and Labrador, Alberta, British Columbia, Nunavut and the Northwest Territories）

有注册资格的地质学家和地球物理学家须完成认证教育规划，具有几年的监督工作经验，以及通过一些省份的专业实践考试。（Geologists and geophysicists are eligible for registration following graduation from an accredited educational program and after several years of supervised work experience and, in some provinces, after passing a professional practice examination）

在某些省份或地区，对于那些没有进行认证教育课程学习的人如果在相关监督工作岗位有6到8年的工作经验，并且顺利通过相关考试的人也有注册资格。In some provinces or territories those who are not graduates of an accredited educational program are eligible for registration after completing a six- to eight-year term of supervised employment and successfully passing examinations. Supervisory and senior positions in this unit group require experience.

加拿大地理学家、地球物理学家主要职责（Main duties）：

地理学家，地球物理学家履行下列的部分或全部职责（Geologists, geochemists and geophysicists perform some or all of the following duties）：

进行行为理论和应用研究以扩大有关地球地表和地下特点的知识，了解其历史和运作的物理，化学和生物系统达到控制演变的作用（Conduct theoretical and applied research to extend knowledge of surface and subsurface features of the earth, its history and the operation of physical, chemical and biological systems that control its evolution)

计划，指导和参与地质，地球化学和地球物理领域的研究以及钻井和地质测试计划（Plan, direct and participate in geological, geochemical and geophysical field studies, drilling and geological testing programs）

计划，指导和参与地震，电磁，磁，重力，辐射，雷达和其他遥感计划（Plan, direct and participate in seismic, electromagnetic, magnetic, gravimetric, radiometric, radar and other remote sensing programs）

计划，指导和参与地质，地球化学和地球物理调查数据，测井和其他检测结果，地图，说明和截面的分析（Plan, direct and participate in the analysis of geological, geochemical and geophysical survey data, well logs and other test results, maps, notes and cross sections）

开发数据判读和分析的应用软件（Develop applied software for the analysis and interpretation of data）

计划，指导和参与岩芯样品，钻屑和岩石样本的分析以确定其化学，矿产，碳氢化合物 和生物方面的组成成分和评估沉积环境和地质年龄（Plan, direct and participate in the analysis of core samples, drill cuttings and rock samples to identify chemical, mineral, hydrocarbon and biological composition and to assess depositional environments and geological age）

评估矿体和碳氢化合物矿床的大小，方向和矿物组成（Assess the size, orientation and composition of mineral ore bodies and hydrocarbon deposits）

确定建筑材料的沉积物以及其特点，判断是否适宜用作混凝土骨料，公路填充料或其他用途（Identify deposits of construction materials and determine their characteristics and suitability for use as concrete aggregates, road fill or for other applications）

评估地面流动和地表水，在废物管理，路线和地点选择和被污染地点恢复等领域提供建议（Assess the movement of ground and surface waters and advise in areas such as waste management, route and site selection and the restoration of contaminated sites）

在申请用地，勘探和测绘项目和矿山发展问题上提出建议（Recommend the acquisition of lands, exploration and mapping programs and mine development）
针对区域发展，选址和公共工程项目的发展进行地质和地球物理研究（Conduct geological and geophysical studies for regional development, site selection and the development of public works projects）

确定和预测自然风险，如坡面侵蚀，滑坡，土壤不稳定，沉陷，地震和火山爆发（Identify and anticipate natural risks such as slope erosion, landslides, soil instability, subsidence, earthquakes and volcanic eruptions）

可能涉及钻井，项目完成情况和检查以及采矿的监督和统筹方面的工作（May supervise and co-ordinate well drilling, completion and work-overs and mining activities）

地质学家可能会专注于以下领域：煤炭地质，环境地质，地质年代学，地貌或地表地质，岩土工程，水文，海洋地质，矿藏或采矿，矿物学，古生物学，石油地质学，岩石学，沉积学，地层学或生物地层，结构地质或构造，火山或其他领域。（Geologists may specialize in the following fields: coal geology, environmental geology, geochronology, geomorphology or surficial geology, geotechnics, hydrogeology, marine geology, mineral deposits or mining, mineralogy, paleontology, petroleum geology, petrology, sedimentology, stratigraphy or biostratigraphy, structural geology or tectonics, volcanology or in other fields.）

地球化学可能会是分析地球化学，水文地球化学，矿产或石油地球化学或其他领域的专家（Geochemists may specialize in analytical geochemistry, hydro geochemistry, mineral or petroleum geochemistry or in other fields）

地球物理学家可能会专门研究地球物理学，海洋学，石油或其他领域（Geophysicists may specialize in earth physics, oceanography, petroleum or in other fields）

附加信息 Additional information

具备多年工作经验后可以在该领域进行具体工作方向的变动
Mobility between specializations in this group is possible with experience.

有机会晋升到采矿，石油等行业的管理职位
Advancement is possible to management positions in mining, petroleum and other industries.

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
