3151 加拿大护士长 Head Nurses and Supervisors

属于加拿大联邦技术移民 227 新政后的38 大类移民紧缺职业（2008.11.28）。

加拿大护士长职责概述

护士长管理和调节注册护士，有实践经验的护士以及其他医护人员的工作。
Head nurses and supervisors supervise and co-ordinate the activities of registered nurses, licensed practical nurses and other nursing personnel in the provision of patient care.

他们受雇于保健机构，如医院，诊所，疗养院和护理机构。They are employed in health care institutions such as hospitals, clinics and nursing homes and in nursing agencies.

加拿大护士长主要职责 Main duties

护士长履行下列的部分或全部职责：Head nurses and supervisors perform some or all of the following duties

主管注册护士，有实践经验的护士以及其他医护人员
Supervise registered nurses, licensed practical nurses and other nursing personnel

评估病人的需要并确保提供到位的护理服务Evaluate patients' needs and ensure that required nursing care is delivered

建立统筹护理服务及其他卫生服务 Set up and co-ordinate nursing services in conjunction with other health services

确保提供有质量的护理服务和合理有序的实施程序Ensure quality nursing care is provided and appropriate administrative procedures are followed

协助制定部门政策和实施步骤Assist in the establishment of unit policies and procedures

管理护理部门预算并确保有必要的物资和设备Administer nursing unit budget and ensure that supplies and equipment are available

协助护理人才的选择，评估和专业发展Assist in the selection, evaluation and professional development of nursing personnel

合作研究有关护理，医疗和多学科服务方面的课题Collaborate on research projects related to nursing and medical care and multidisciplinary services

可能会直接护理病人May provide direct patient care.

加拿大护士长就业要求及移民条件 Employment Requirements

要求必需完成了大学，学院，或其他通过批准的注册护理专业的学习。Completion of a university, college or other approved registered nursing program is required.

管理课程的学习，比如加拿大医协会提供的护理管理课程，或具有行政管理的学位，文凭，证书或相关专业的学习等。Courses in management studies such as the Nursing Unit Administration Course offered by the Canadian Hospital Association or other degree, diploma, certificate or studies in management or administration may be required

必须持有由省或地区检察机关颁发的护士注册证，在马尼托巴省；萨斯喀彻温省，阿尔伯塔省和不列颠哥伦比省这些地区需是注册的精神科护士。Registration as a registered nurse by a provincial or territorial regulatory body or, in Manitoba, Saskatchewan, Alberta and British Columbia, provincial registration as a registered psychiatric nurse is required.

注册的护士须有临床经验。Clinical experience as a registered nurse is required.

附加信息 Additional information

具备一定经验后可以晋升为护士长等管理职位。Progression to management positions, such as director of nursing, is possible with experience.

其他分类 Classified elsewhere

护理主任和护理管理员（见 0311 医疗主任）Directors of nursing and nursing unit administrators (in 0311 Managers in Health Care)

如果您的职业不在加拿大联邦技术移民紧缺职业名单中，您可以考虑是否可以申请其他类别的移民：

1. 可以考虑学习法语申请魁北克技术移民；
2. 跟进省提名政策，机会成熟时申请加拿大省提名技术移民；
3. 申请工作签证，2年后申请经验类移民；
4. 申请留学，3年后申请经验类移民；
5. 如果加拿大有亲属，可以考虑申请该亲属所在省份的省提名亲属类移民（BC 与 ON 省除外）；
6. 如果对澳洲感兴趣，也可以考虑是否符合澳洲技术移民的申请条件 ；
7. 如果资金充足可以考虑留学，对于事业有成者也可以考虑商业移民：投资，企业家及自雇移民。
