加拿大技术移民香港使馆表格填写简单说明和下载地址

1. APPLICATION FOR PERMANENT RESIDENCE IN CANADA (IMM0008) （主申请）
Completed by the principal applicant

A. Mailing and residential addresses - items 14 and 15:
. The mailing address you provide must be the residence or business address of a person or a firm. A post office box number is not acceptable.
. 8 self-addressed mailing labels bearing applicant’s name, mailing address and postal code in Chinese characters, if applicable
. Residential and mailing addresses in the PRC must be provided in Chinese as well as English, and must include a postal code.
. If you have a designated a third party to represent you, we will communicate only with that person and will automatically direct all correspondence to this person.
. It is your responsibility to ensure that the mailing address is reliable, and that any changes are reported to us promptly. Failure to do so could result in significant delays in the processing of your application or even refusal of your application for non-compliance with our instructions.

B. Photographs
You are required to submit 6 photos of yourself and each of your family members, whether they are accompanying you to Canada or not. The requirements for the photos are indicated on Appendix C. Place all your photos in an envelope. Please do not staple the photos.

2. SCHEDULE 1: BACKGROUND / DECLARATION （主、副申请及 18 岁上的受供养子女）
Completed and signed by:
. the principal applicant
. spouse or common-law partner
. each dependent child 18 years of age and over

3. SUPPLEMENT TO SCHEDULE 1 - BACKGROUND / DECLARATION . （副申请及 18 岁上的受供养子女）
Completed and signed by:
. spouse or common-law partner
. each dependent child 18 years of age and over

4. SCHEDULE 3: ECONOMIC CLASSES – FEDERAL SKILLED WORKERS （主申请）
Completed by the principal applicant

5. SCHEDULE 5: DECLARATION OF INTENT TO RESIDE IN QUEBEC （主申请，目的地是魁北克的申请者需要）
Completed and signed by the principal applicant, if your destination in Canada is the province of Quebec

6. ADDITIONAL FAMILY INFORMATION (IMM5406) （主、副申请及 18 岁上的受供养子女）
Completed and signed by:
. the principal applicant
. spouse or common-law partner
. each dependent child 18 years of age and over

7. USE OF A REPRESENTATIVE (IMM5476) （代理律师）
Include this form if you have obtained help from a representative to complete your application or if you wish us to release information regarding your application to someone other than yourself. For complete information, please refer to the ‘Immigration Representatives’ page on our web site:
www.cic.gc.ca/english/information/representative/index.asp
