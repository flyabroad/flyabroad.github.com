加拿大新移民五年内住满两年的居住要求

居住时间如何计算

一个永久居民，在任何一个五年时间以内，必须有两年的时间居住在加拿大。
这两年得时间是如何计算的呢，有以下几种情况：
1) 本人在加拿大居住（只是居住，没有其他额外要求）；
2) 陪伴有加拿大国籍的配偶或父母在海外居住的时间也可以算在内。(注意，在海外陪伴有加拿大国籍的子女则不能算)；
3) 为一家驻外的加拿大公司或加拿大政府机构全职雇佣。
4) 陪伴一个为一家驻外的加拿大公司或加拿大政府机构全职雇佣的具有永久居民身份的配偶或子女（该永久居民也要符合上述5年内住满2年的要求）。

也就是说除了本人实际居住还有其他的 移民监 方式，最主要的是要证明自己是税务居民，并且有在加拿大长久居住的意向。否则只想享受加拿大的福利和医疗，不为加拿大做恭喜是不行的。

相关英文解释

Permanent residents comply with residency obligations if they accumulate two years of physical presence in Canada in every five-year period. If they are outside of Canada for extended periods of time, they can accumulate residency days if they are:

  • accompanying aCanadian spouse or common-law partner, or are a child accompanying aparent;
  • employed on afull-time basis by a Canadian business or the Public Service of Canada;or
  • the accompanyingspouse, common-law partner or child of a permanent resident who is outsideCanada and who is employed on a full-time basis by a Canadian businessor the Public Service of Canada.

Under the transitional provisions, a permanent resident in possession of a Returning Resident Permit will be able to count time spent outside Canada for the purpose of satisfying residency obligations.

When making residency decisions, officers can take into account humanitarian and compassionate concerns. Officers will consider the best interests of a child who would be affected by the decision.

The assessment of residency obligations for a person who has been a permanent resident for more than five years, will be limited to the five years immediately preceding the examination.
