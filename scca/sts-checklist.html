加拿大短期学生临时居留签证申请材料清单（申请人学习六个月或六个月以下）
Checklist for Short-Term Student Temporary Resident Visa Application (Applicant to study for 6 months or less)

1.
Original passport or travel document including at least one blank page, and valid for at least one month from the date you intend to leave Canada.
至少有一页空白页的护照或旅行证件原件，此证件有效期至少要持续到申请人计划从加拿大离境后的一个月

2.
Four passport-size photos (colour or black and white) taken within the past 6 months and including the date of birth (day/month/year) and applicant’s name clearly printed in pinyin on the back of each photo.
四张在最近六个月内拍摄的护照相尺寸的照片（彩色或黑白）并用清晰的印刷体拼音在每张照片后面注明申请人姓名及出生日期（日/月/年）

3.
Correct application processing fee.
正确的申请受理费

4.
Two self-addressed adhesive labels per person, including the applicant’s name, address and postal code in Chinese characters.
两份用中文写有申请人姓名，地址及邮编的粘性贴纸

5.
Two signed (original signature) and dated copies of “Application for Temporary Resident Visa” Form (English/French), including your employer’s telephone and fax numbers in box 10.
两份申请人签名（原始签名）、注明日期并在第十栏写明申请人雇主电话及传真号的“临时居住签证表”（英文/法文）

6.
One signed (original signature) and dated copy of the “Family Composition/Education and Employment” form (Chinese and English/French).
一份申请人签字（原始签名）并注明日期的家属表及教育和就业细节表格（中文及英文/法文）

7.
For organized study groups, invitation letter with details of the program, list of participants and an indication of who will be paying associated fees and expenses.
若为团体学习团组，包含项目详情、参与人员名单及所涉及哪方支付相关费用的邀请信。

8.
One original letter of acceptance from the Canadian school the applicant is going to attend. The letter should include the following information:
a. how long the applicant is going to attend the courses.
b. the subjects of the courses the applicant is going to attend.
c. how much the tuition fee is and if it has been paid already by the applicant.
申请人计划在加学习的学校出具的录取通知书原件，应包含以下信息：
a. 申请人计划学习的课程时限
b. 申请人计划学习的所有课程科目
c. 相应学习费用及申请人是否已支付

9.
Letter of Consent authorizing travel for children under the age of 18 years.
十八岁以下的申请人须有父母同意其旅行的同意函

10.
A completed form “Use of a Representative” if applicable.
一份填写完整的代理人信息表（如适用）

11.
A signed original parental employment letters issued by the employer on company letterhead indicating position, salary and date of hire.
父母双方雇主分别签发的带有公司抬头的在职证明原件，注明职务、薪金和起雇日期

12.
Bank statements showing current savings with at least 12 months history.
至少有十二个月存款历史的银行对账单

13.
Notarized copy of the highest diploma obtained by the applicant.
申请人获得的最高学历的公证件

Note/注意:
If you intend to travel to the United States during your visit to Canada, you do not need a multiple-entry visa in order to re-enter Canada directly from the United States during the period for which you were originally admitted to Canada.

如果申请人想在访问加拿大期间前往美国，在加拿大合法停留期限之内从美国再次进入加拿大不需要一个多次入境签证。

Please note that where a multiple entry visa is paid for but, at the discretion of the visa officer, a single-entry visa is issued, the fee difference will not be refunded. Moreover, the processing fee is non-refundable for any application that is withdrawn or refused.

请注意，如果支付了多次入境签证费用、但是签证官只考虑签发了一个单次入境签证，费用差异不予退还。并且，任何撤销或被拒签的申请受理费也不予退还。
