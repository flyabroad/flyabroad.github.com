VETASSESS 职业评估机构介绍 - FLYabroad

VETASSESS负责领导澳大利亚的职业教育和培训机构。帮助全世界的个人、培训机构、集团明确并达到他们的学习和评估目标。 VETASSESS is Australia’s leading vocational education and training (VET) assessment provider. We help individuals, training providers and industry groups around the world define and achieve their learning and assessment goals.

VETASSESS 职业评估机构评估的申请条件 - FLYabroad

申请技工职业 Applying for a trade occupation，as long as you are applying for migration under either

* 一般技术移民 General Skilled Migration (GSM)
* 雇主提名 Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS)
* 临时商业赞助移民 Temporary Business (Long Stay) Standard Business Sponsorship (Subclass 457) visa

申请一般职业 Applying for a general professional occupation

* 申请澳大利亚GSM、ENS或毕业生临时签证 currently be a permanent resident of a country other than Australia and wish to migrate to Australia under the General Skilled Migration (GSM) or the Employer Nomination Scheme (ENS) program, or be applying for a Skilled – Graduate (Temporary) visa (subclass 485)
* 提名一个一般职业，如管理类、技师类、社区、个人服务、文职、行政或销售 nominate a general professional occupation – managerial, technician, community, personal service, clerical, administrative or sales occupation for which VETASSESS is the relevant assessing authority.
* 中学或更高学历，与提名职业相关领域 hold post-secondary or higher educational qualifications at the required educational level, in a field highly relevant to your nominated occupation * 过去五年中有一年高度相关职业工作经验have at least one year of employment experience out of the last five in a highly relevant field to your nominated occupation (unless you need an assessment for 485 visa purposes in which case employment is not assessed).

VETASSESS 职业评估机构评估申请类型分组 - FLYabroad

Group A 要求：澳大利亚学历框架AQF学士学位水平或更高（Bachelor degree），且专业是与提名职业高度相关的领域； 递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有1年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后，毕业前的工作经验不予考虑），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当。

Group B 要求：澳大利亚学历框架AQF学士学位水平或更高（Bachelor degree），且专业是与提名职业高度相关的领域； 递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有1年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当。毕业前经验也在考虑之内。如果你的专业不是与提名职业高度相关领域的，则需要增加工作经验要求。
1.高度相关专业：要求1年以上高度相关领域工作经验（递交申请前的五年中、适当的技能水平）
2.与AQF文凭、AQF高级文凭、澳大利亚副学士学位或澳大利亚毕业证书水平相当的、高度相关专业的其他证书(with another qualification with a highly relevant major assessed at either AQF Diploma, AQF Advanced Diploma, Australian Associate Degree or Australian Graduate Diploma level):要求2年以上高度相关领域工作经验（递交申请前的五年中、适当的技能水平）
3.其他情况：要求3年以上高度相关领域工作经验（递交申请前的五年中、适当的技能水平）

Group C 要求：等同于澳大利亚学历框架毕业文凭或更高（Diploma），与提名职业高度相关专业；递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有一年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当。毕业前经验也在考虑之内。若专业与提名职业不相关，需要两年以上上述工作经验。

Group D 要求：等同于澳大利亚学历框架AQF三级或四级证书（Certificate III or IV），高度相关专业；工作经验要求如下（毕业后的）：
AQF 四级水平:递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有一年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当；如果专业不是高度相关的话，需要两年工作经验（ Two years of such employment is required if the qualification/s has a shortfall in content relevance.）
AQF 三级水平:递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有三年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当；工作经验无法代替专业不足

Group E 要求：等同于澳大利亚学历框架毕业文凭（Diploma,），高度相关专业；递交技术评估前的过去五年中，有一年以上同提名职业高度相关的领域的工作经验（毕业后，毕业前工作经验不予考虑），且与提名职业要求的技能水平相当

VETASSESS 职业评估机构处理周期 - FLYabroad

技工类：申请457签证5-7周；永久技术移民评估师6-10周 For a temporary 457 visa skills assessment, the assessment is normally completed within 5-7 weeks. For permanent migration skills assessment it may take 6 to 10. This can vary depending on whether a technical interview or in country assessment is conducted.

一般职业：485类签证是8周，GSM/ ENSOL/ State Sponsored visa 类技术评估师8-12周
Skilled Graduate Visa (485) Please note that the processing time for a qualifications only assessment (485 visas) is approximately 8 weeks once all documents required for assessment have been received by VETASSESS. Some cases may take longer depending on the complexity level of the case. GSM/ ENSOL/ State Sponsored visa If you apply for a full skills assessment to SRGO, the approximate time for assessment is 8-12 weeks once all documents required for assessment have been received by VETASSESS. Some cases may take longer depending on the complexity level of the case. If you have a full skills Assessment which is now urgent due to your visa expiring or upcoming birthday, please submit an urgency request form and we will attempt to meet your deadline. Upcoming migration changes and potential State/Territory Sponsorship changes are not usually sufficient for an application to be processed urgently.
