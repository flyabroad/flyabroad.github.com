ANZSCO 3211 汽车电工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 使用测试设备查找电气电子故障 using test equipment to locate electrical and electronic malfunctions
• 拆除和移除电气电子组件和部件 dismantling and removing electrical and electronic assemblies and components
• 安装电气设备和汽车的电子元件 installing electrical equipment and electronic components in motor vehicles
• 机动车辆设备及配件供应 connecting power-operated vehicle equipment and accessories to power supply
• 调整发动机控制系统并定时 adjusting engine control systems and timing
• 测试并更换故障的交流发电机、发电机、电压调节器和电动机起动器 testing and replacing defective alternators, generators, voltage regulators and starter motors
• 修理并更换有问题的点火装置和电线 repairing and replacing faulty ignition and electrical wiring
• 换有缺陷的部件如保险丝、灯具和开关 replacing defective parts such as fuses, lamps and switches
