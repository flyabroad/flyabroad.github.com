ANZSCO 32 汽车与工程行业工人职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 汽车与工程行业工人构造、修理和维护机动车辆与飞机的结构和系统；切割、塑型、浇注、连接和装饰完成金属、金属部件、组件和精密仪器。 AUTOMOTIVE AND ENGINEERING TRADES WORKERS construct, repair and maintain motor vehicles and aircraft structures and systems, and cut, shape, cast, join and finish metal, metal parts, subassemblies and precision instruments.

ANZSCO 32 汽车与工程行业工人技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 32 汽车与工程行业工人主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 诊断机动车辆与飞机电气和机械故障 diagnosing electrical and mechanical faults in motor vehicles and aircraft
• 拆卸发动机与电子设备、移除损害仪表板与内部装饰 dismantling engines and electrical systems, and removing damaged panels and interior trim
• 维修、更换破损部件 repairing and replacing worn and defective parts
• 为修理好的汽车表层喷漆 painting repaired vehicle surfaces
• 构造、修改、绘画、装饰原型与特殊汽车车身 constructing, modifying, painting and upholstering prototype and specialised vehicle bodies
• 标记、切割金属 marking and cutting out metal stock
• 手工或用机床加工机械 shaping stock using hand and machine tools
• 用模具里塑造金属制品 shaping metal in moulds
• 通过焊接、钎焊、铆接、栓接等办法连接材料与部件 joining stock and components by welding, soldering, brazing, riveting, bolting, screwing and gluing
• 安装、测试部件，装饰金属产品 fitting and testing components, and applying protective and decorative finishes to metal products
