ANZSCO 3231 飞机维修工程师职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 维护并修理飞机结构、航电及机械系统。AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE ENGINEERS maintain and repair aircraft structures, and avionic and mechanical systems.

ANZSCO 3231 飞机维修工程师技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有与下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF证书3级，包括至少2年的在职培训或AQF证书IV级（ANZSCO技术3级）In Australia: AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰：NNZ资格证4级（ANZSCO技术3级）In New Zealand: NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 至少3年的工作经验可以代替上述的资格证。At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above.
• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3231 飞机维修工程师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 拆卸、检查、测试、修理和重装飞机发动机、辅助发动机、发动机配件、电气系统和机体的组件。dismantling, inspecting, testing, repairing and reassembling aircraft engines, ancillary motors and engine accessories, electrical systems, and subassemblies of aircraft frames
• 安装电路和电气设备。 installing electrical circuits and equipment
• 采用电子测试设备和专业检测仪器来测试飞机通讯设备、仪表和电子系统。 testing aircraft communication equipment, aircraft instrumentation and electronic systems using electronic testing equipment and specialised test apparatus
• 更换并测试飞机供氧系统的各组件。 replacing and testing aircraft oxygen system components
• 组装飞机机体各部件。 assembling parts and subassemblies of aircraft frames
• 进行发动机、飞机机体和机械系统飞行前例行检查。 conducting routine pre-flight inspections of engines, aircraft frames and mechanical systems
• 记录采取措施。 maintaining records of action taken
• 可能要制造飞机的电气、仪器和无线电硬件组件。 may manufacture aircraft electrical, instrument and radio hardware components
