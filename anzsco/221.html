ANZSCO 221 会计师，审计师和公司秘书职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 会计师，审计师和公司秘书主要负责为个人和机构制定财政计划，进行财务核算和财政评估。并计划和审查其活动是否符合法律规定。ACCOUNTANTS, AUDITORS AND COMPANY SECRETARIES plan and provide accounting, financial auditing and treasury valuation services and systems to individuals and organisations, and plan and review legislative compliance activities.

ANZSCO 221 会计师，审计师和公司秘书的主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 收集，分析和说明有关机构的财政状况, 成本结构和机构的贸易可行性方面的信息 collecting, analysing and interpreting information on the financial status, cost structures and trading effectiveness of organizations
• 计划，重组并进行预算成本控制和建立其他会计系统，如计算机系统 devising, re-organising and establishing budgetary cost control and other accounting systems such as computer-based systems
• 进行财务审计和调查并为管理人，股东，法人或其他主体准备财务报表和财务报告 conducting audits and investigations and preparing financial statements and reports for management, shareholders, and statutory and other bodies
• 管理财务和财务系统并建立和评估风险管理目标和财务政策 controlling treasury and treasury systems and establishing and reviewing risk management objectives and treasury policies
• 安排相关会议并对董事和股东进行会议通知 arranging and giving notice of meetings of directors and shareholders
• 对机构董事会在遵从交易所牌价规则，相关立法和公司惯例方面提出建议 advising organizations' governing boards on matters concerning compliance with stock exchange listing rules, relevant legislation and corporation practice
