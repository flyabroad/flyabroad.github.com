ANZSCO 33 砖匠和石匠主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad
• 研究图纸和计划方案，估计所需材料的数量。studying drawings and plans, and estimating quantities of materials required
• 砌砖，摊开连接处间的砂浆，移除多余的砂浆laying bricks and blocks, spreading mortar between joints, and removing excess mortar
• 安装框架、屋顶结构、屋顶和墙壁。erecting frameworks, roof frames, roofs and walls
• 铺设地板、地毯和瓷砖。laying flooring, carpets and tiles
• 为表层上漆和贴壁纸。painting and wallpapering surfaces
• 将石膏和水泥涂在墙壁表层。applying plaster and cement coatings to walls
• 建立和安装配件。building and installing fittings
• 制作和安装玻璃制品。making and installing glass products
• 安装水管和排水系统，installing plumbing and drainage systems, guttering and other rainwater systems
• 安装煤气用具和空调管道。installing gas appliances and airconditioning piping and ducting
