ANZSCO 3212 汽车机械师职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 汽车机械师修理、维护并测试机动车以及其他内燃机和相关机械部件。机动车零件修理工和零配件装配工属于代码8994而非此组。 MOTOR MECHANICS repair, maintain and test motor vehicle and other internal combustion engines and related mechanical components. Motor Vehicle Parts and Accessories Fitters are excluded from this unit group. Motor Vehicle Parts and Accessories Fitters are included in Unit Group 8994 Motor Vehicle Parts and Accessories Fitters.

ANZSCO 3212 汽车机械师技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本职业需要如下条件 Most occupations in this sub-major group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.
• 在澳大利亚： In Australia:
• AQF3级证书加2年以上在职培训或AQF4级证书（ANZSCO 3级水平）。 AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰： In New Zealand:
• NZ4级注册证书（ANZSCO 3级水平）。NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 三年以上相关经验可以代替上方所示正规资格。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和/或在职培训经历。需要注册或许可证。 At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. Registration or licensing may be required.

ANZSCO 3212 汽车机械师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 发动机和零部件的故障检测和诊断 detecting and diagnosing faults in engines and parts
• 拆除发动机装配、变速器、转向装置和其他组件并检查部件 dismantling and removing engine assemblies, transmissions, steering mechanisms and other components, and checking parts
• 修理和更换磨损部件，进行机械部件重新组装，并根据需要设计服务手册 repairing and replacing worn and defective parts and reassembling mechanical components, and referring to service manuals as needed
• 执行预定维修服务，例如更换机油、润滑油、调整发动机以实现车辆的顺畅运行并确保车辆符合环境保护条例 performing scheduled maintenance services, such as oil changes, lubrications and engine tune-ups, to achieve smoother running of vehicles and ensure compliance with pollution regulations
• 维修后重装引擎和零件 reassembling engines and parts after being repaired
• 维修后测试并调整机械部件以确保汽车性能 testing and adjusting mechanical parts after being repaired for proper performance
• 借助电脑诊断并测试零件 diagnosing and testing parts with the assistance of computers
• 可能检查车辆、颁发性能证书或制定具体工作要求以实现车辆性能 may inspect vehicles and issue roadworthiness certificates or detail work required to achieve roadworthiness
