ANZSCO 1411 咖啡店和餐厅经理主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 与厨师磋商制定菜单 planning menus in consultation with Chefs
• 规划和组织特殊事件 planning and organising special functions
• 根据预算安排商品采购和商品定价 arranging the purchasing and pricing of goods according to budget
• 维护库存水平和财务交易记录 maintaining records of stock levels and financial transactions
• 保证餐饮设施遵守健康条例，干净整洁 ensuring dining facilities comply with health regulations and are clean, functional and of suitable appearance
• 听取顾客意见，评估他们对饭菜和服务的满意度 conferring with customers to assess their satisfaction with meals and service
• 挑选、培训和管理侍者和厨房员工 selecting, training and supervising waiting and kitchen staff
• 可能负责预定、欢迎客人，帮助他们下单 may take reservations, greet guests and assist in taking orders
