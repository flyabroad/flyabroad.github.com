ANZSCO 3222 板金技工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 研究图纸和说明书，以确定工作职责、需要的材料和设备 studying blueprints, drawings and specifications to determine job, material and equipment requirements
• 选择金属原料，如不锈钢、镀锌铁、碳钢、铝、铜等，并检查仪器及其他金属原来的规格尺寸 selecting metal stock, such as stainless steel, galvanised iron, mild steel, aluminium and copper, and checking sizes, gauges and other dimensions of metal stock against specifications
• 使用模板、仪表及其他计量器具标出金属材料的参照点和线条 marking out metal stock with reference points and lines, using templates, gauges and other measuring instruments
• 根据使用指南用手动及电动剪、切纸机切割金属原料 cutting metal stock along guidelines using hand and power shears, guillotines and drills
• 使用折叠和弯曲机、压路机、切割机和锤子切割定型成产品 shaping and forming cut metal stock into products using folding and bending machines, rollers, presses and hammers
• 通过铆接、焊接、钎焊、铜焊和其他连接物等将组装元件为成品 fitting and assembling components into final products by welding, riveting, soldering, brazing and otherwise joining
• 用抛光整理、锉削、打磨和清洁，对产品进行精加工 finishing products by polishing, filing, sanding and cleaning assembled products
• 可能修复损坏的钣金产品和零件 may repair damaged sheetmetal products and components
• 可能专门从事制造、或现场组装和安装钣金产品 may specialise in fabrication, or on-site assembly and installation, of sheetmetal products
• 需要先进的绘图和计算技巧，来可能生产飞机的钣金零件 may produce aircraft sheet metal components requiring advanced drawing and calculating skills
• 可能专职做装潢 may specialise in decorative copperwork
