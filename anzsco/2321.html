ANZSCO 2321 建筑师和造园技师主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 向客户和管理部门提供建议并确定已规划建筑和现有建筑改造的类型、风格及规模 obtaining advice from clients and management to determine type, style and size of planned buildings and alterations to existing buildings
• 提供设计、材料及预算建筑期方面的信息 providing information regarding designs, materials and estimated building times
• 准备项目文件包括草图和比例图，在最终的设计里整合结构、力及审美等元素 preparing project documentation, including sketches and scale drawings, and integrating structural, mechanical and aesthetic elements in final designs
• 撰写技术规范和合同文件供承建商和投标人员使用 writing specifications and contract documents for use by builders and calling tenders on behalf of clients
• 与客户和技术人员协商外区设计、成本及建造 consulting with Professionals and clients about external area designs, costs and construction
• 撰写并分析有关地理和生态特征、地貌、土壤、植被、基地水文、视觉特性及人工结构等方面的现场和社区资料，给予土地使用与开发的建议，准备环境影响评介 compiling and analysing site and community data about geographical and ecological features, landforms, soils, vegetation, site hydrology, visual characteristics and human-made structures, to formulate land use and development recommendations, and for preparing environmental impact statements
• 为土地开发准备报告，总设计图，施工图，技术规范及成本估算。展示设计细节包括地面造型、结构、植被及土地使用 preparing reports, site plans, working drawings, specifications and cost estimates for land development, showing location and details of proposals, including ground modelling, structures, vegetation and access
• 监督施工进程确保符合规划，技术规范和质量标准 inspecting construction work in progress to ensure compliance with plans, specifications and quality standards
