ANZSCO 61 销售代表和代理职责概述 - FLYabroad

•代表公司销售产品和服务；代理客户出售房产和其它物业。 SALES REPRESENTATIVES AND AGENTS represent companies in selling their goods and services, and sell real estate and other property on behalf of clients
•ICT和技术销售代表不在此子类中。ICT销售代表包含在2252类 ICT专业销售员内。技术销售代表包含在2254类中。ICT and Technical Sales Representatives are excluded from this sub-major groupICT Sales Representatives are included in Unit Group 2252 ICT Sales ProfessionalsTechnical Sales Representatives are included in Unit Group 2254 Technical Sales Representatives

ANZSCO 61 销售代表和代理主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

•推销和销售产品和服务。promoting and selling goods and services, properties and businesses
•吸引潜在的客户。 engaging prospective clients
•确定潜在客户的需求并解释何类产品、服务和房产可满足其需求。determining the needs of prospective clients and explaining which goods, services and properties meet their needs
•访问客户以建立销售机会。visiting clients to establish selling opportunities
•回访客户，并估计客户对购买产品和服务的满意度。following up clients and gauging satisfaction with goods and services purchased
•监控客户变更的需求和竞争者的行动。monitoring clients' changing needs and competitor activity
