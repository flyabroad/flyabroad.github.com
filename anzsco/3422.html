ANZSCO 3422 配电行业技工职责概述 - FLYabroad

• ELECTRICAL DISTRIBUTION TRADES WORKERS prepare, install, repair, maintain and patrol electric power distribution networks

ANZSCO 3422 配电行业技工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 安装导管和空中设备、地下电缆和设备。installing conductors and aerial equipment, and underground cables and equipment
• 安装和维护与供电有关的设备，如变压器。installing and maintaining equipment associated with electrical supply such as transformers
• 处理电击穿和紧急情况。attending to electrical breakdown and emergencies
• 维护电杆和相关的金属附件、供电和路灯的持续性。maintaining poles and associated hardware, and continuity of electrical supply and street lighting
• 执行空中和地下供电网络的日常维修。conducting routine maintenance on the aerial and underground electricity supply network
• 执行低压切换操作。conducting low-voltage switching operations
• 装配电杆和横木。fitting pole hardware and crossarms
• 在连接和安装电气设备和架空线时，准备高低压电缆接头和电缆封端。preparing low- and high-voltage cable joints and cable terminations while connecting and installing electrical equipment and overhead lines
• 使用重工厂设备，如架空工作台和便携设备。using heavy plant equipment such as elevated work platforms and portable equipment such as hydraulic drills
• 可能要进行变电站安装和维护、特定测试和仪表安装。may undertake substation installation and maintenance, and specialised testing and revenue meter installation
