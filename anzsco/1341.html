ANZSCO 1341 幼儿中心管理人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 幼儿中心管理人员主要负责企划、组织、指导、控制和协调幼儿中心的活动，以及物质和人力资源服务。CHILD CARE CENTRE MANAGERS plan, organise, direct, control and coordinate the activities of childcare centres and services including physical and human resources.

ANZSCO 1341 幼儿中心管理人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 1341 幼儿中心管理人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 制定并实施方案，以提高幼儿的体质、社交能力、情绪和智力发展 developing and implementing programs to enhance the physical, social, emotional and intellectual development of young children
• 提供孩子学前、课后、日间的看护，以及度假中心的孩子看护 providing care for children in before-school, after-school, day, and vacation care centres
• 指导和监督儿童看护人员对幼儿的看护工作 directing and supervising Child Carers in providing care and supervision for young children
• 保证中心的孩子、员工和游客的安全 ensuring the centre is a safe area for children, staff and visitors
• 遵守政府有关规定和标准 complying with relevant government requirements and standards
• 与家长保持交流 liaising with parents
• 做好中心帐目登记 maintaining records and accounts for the centre
• 招聘员工并协调专业人员的发展 recruiting staff and coordinating professional development
