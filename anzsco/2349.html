ANZSCO 2349 其他自然和物理科学专业人士职业描述 - FLYabroad

• 这个职业类别包含了其他地方没有分类的自然和物理科学人士。包括了自然保护者，冶金学家，气象学家和物理学家 This unit group covers Natural and Physical Science Professionals not elsewhere classified.
It includes Conservators, Metallurgists, Meteorologists and Physicists.

ANZSCO 2349 其他自然和物理科学专业人士技能要求 - FLYabroad

• 该类别下的大多职位要求学士学位或更高学历。有些情况下，除了正规学历还要求相关工作经验或在职培训。对于自然保护者，至少5年的相关经验才能代替正式的资质。可能需要注册 或工作许可（AZNSCO 1级）Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. In the case of Conservators, at least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).Registration or licensing may be required.

234911 自然保护者 Conservator

• 计划并组织保护图书馆，档案馆，博物馆，美术馆和其他机构的材料和对象 Plans and organises the conservation of materials and objects in libraries, archives, museums, • art galleries and other institutions.
• 技能水平：1级 Skill Level: 1
• 具体职业 Specialisation:
• 艺术保护者 Art Conservator

234912 冶金学家 Metallurgist

• 开发，控制，提供建议给从矿石中提取金属，铸造，合金化，热处理，焊接金属和合金以及其他材料研究，从而生产商业金属制品或开发新的冶金和过程。可能需要注册或工作许可。 Researches, develops, controls and provides advice on processes used in extracting metals from their ores, and processes used for casting, alloying, heat treating or welding refined metals, alloys and other materials to produce commercial metal products or develop new alloys and processes. Registration or licensing may be required.
• 技能水平：1级 Skill Level: 1
• 具体职业 Specialisations:
• 湿法冶炼工艺工程师 Hydrometallurgical Engineer
• 冶金工程师 Metallographer
• 高温冶金在处理工程师 Pyrometallurgical Engineer
• 放射学工程师 Radiological Metallurgist

234913 气象学家 Meteorologist

• 研究大气的物理与动力来增加对天气和气候的理解并预测天气变化和长期的气候趋势变化 Studies the physics and dynamics of the atmosphere to increase understanding of • weather and climate, and to forecast changes in the weather and long-term climatic trends.
• 技能水平：1级 Skill Level: 1
• 具体职业 Specialisations:
• 气候学家 Climatologist
• 天气预报员 Weather Forecaster

234914 物理学家 PHYSICIST

• 研究物质，空间，时间，能量，力量和领域，以及这些物理现象之间的相互关系以更深的理解控制宇宙行为的法则，将这些法则应用到解决实际问题，并发现地球和宇宙的新信息 Studies matter, space, time, energy, forces and fields and the interrelationship between these physical phenomena to further understanding of the laws governing the behaviour of the universe, and seeks to apply these laws to solve practical problems and discover new information about the earth and the universe.
• 技能水平：1级 Skill Level: 1
• 具体职业 Specialisations:
• 天文学家 Astronomer
• 医学物理师 Medical Physicist
