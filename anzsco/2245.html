ANZSCO 2245 土地经济学家和估价员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 土地经济学家和估价员主要负责对土地和资产的管理和使用提供建议，并评估土地，房产和其他资产的价值，如商业设备和美术馆。LAND ECONOMISTS AND VALUERS provide advice on the administration and use of land and property, and assess the value of land, property and other items such as commercial equipment and objects of art.

ANZSCO 2245 土地经济学家和估价员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 对土地和房产的融资和股价问题提供建议 providing advice on land and property financing and valuation matters
• 对土地和资产的管理和使用进行研究和建议 researching and advising on the administration and use of land and property
• 完善和执行商业土地和资产的销售和租赁提案 developing and implementing sales and leasing proposals for commercial land and property
• 为土地和资产的管理和使用提供财产管理服务 providing asset management services for the administration and use of land and property
• 分析土地和房产投资 analysing land and property investments
• 管理土地和资产投资组合以及商业资产的开发 managing land and property portfolios and commercial property developments
• 考虑市场需求，项目条件，未来趋势和其他因素计算价值 calculating values by considering market demand, condition of items, future trends and other factors
• 审核财产，选择评估方式并提交书面评估报告 examining property, selecting methods of valuation, and submitting written assessments
• 为合法的诉讼程序，价值的评估和为仲裁而提交的租赁提供证据 giving evidence in legal proceedings, mediating on valuation matters and providing rental determinations for arbitration purposes
