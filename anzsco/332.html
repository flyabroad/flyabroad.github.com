ANZSCO 332 地面铺设工和油漆工职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 安装和修理软地板和弹性地板；使用颜料、清漆、壁纸和其它罩面漆来保护、维护和装饰建筑表面。FLOOR FINISHERS AND PAINTING TRADES WORKERS install and repair soft and resilient floor coverings, and apply paint, varnish, wallpaper and other finishes to protect, maintain and decorate the surfaces of buildings and structures

ANZSCO 332 地面铺设工和油漆工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 测量要覆盖的区域，计划、商议并确定地板材料的数量。measuring areas to be covered and consulting plans to estimate quantities of floor covering materials required
• 去除旧油漆和壁纸，固定木制品，填充空洞和裂缝，打磨并密封表面。preparing surfaces by removing old paint and wallpaper, fixing woodwork, filling holes and cracks, and smoothing and sealing surfaces
• 测量、切割并安装衬底材料。measuring, cutting and fixing underlay materials
• 铺设衬底和覆盖材料，拼接图案，切割图形并修边。laying underlay and covering materials on floors, matching patterns, cutting shapes around fixtures and trimming edges
• 通过混合颜料来准备所需颜色的油漆，稀释并干燥添加剂。selecting and preparing paints to required colours by mixing portions of pigment, oil, and thinning and drying additives
• 使用刷子、滚筒和喷剂将颜料、清漆和染色剂涂在表层。applying paints, varnishes and stains to surfaces using brushes, rollers and sprays
