ANZSCO 25 医疗健康专家职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 健康专家制定卫生保健方案和政策，对生理疾病进行试验、诊断和治疗，为病人提供护理建议和咨询，维护、提升、恢复身体健康和安全的工作环境。 HEALTH PROFESSIONALS develop health care programs and policies, conduct tests and diagnose and treat physical and physiological disorders, and provide nursing care, advice and counselling to patients to maintain, promote and restore good health and safe and healthy working environments.

ANZSCO 25 医疗健康专家技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 In Australia and New Zealand: Most occupations in this sub-major group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 25 医疗健康专家主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 检查病人，找出病因，确定治疗程序 examining patients to establish the nature of their complaint and performing or ordering diagnostic procedures
• 选择适合的治疗方法 selecting and administering appropriate treatment, medication and therapy
• 规定康复和矫正设备 prescribing prosthetic and corrective devices
• 提出治疗措施、康复方法和护理服务 providing remedial therapy, rehabilitation and nursing care
• 建议为个人、社区提供人口保健措施、健康提升和安全的工作环境 advising on individual, community and population health measures and health promotion, and safe working environments
