ANZSCO 134 教育、健康和福利服务管理人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 教育、健康和福利服务管理人负责企划、组织、指导、控制和协调儿童保育、健康、福利和教育服务的条款。EDUCATION, HEALTH AND WELFARE SERVICES MANAGERS plan, organise, direct, control and coordinate the provision of childcare, health, welfare and education services.

ANZSCO 134 教育、健康和福利服务管理人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本类大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验可以代替正规资格要求。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历。（ANZSCO 1级水平） Most occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 134 教育、健康和福利服务管理人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 为儿童保育、健康、福利和教育机构制定政策、计划、标准和目标 formulating policies and plans for the operation of childcare, health, welfare and educational establishments, and setting standards and objectives
• 制定并实施计划和服务来满足客户的需求 developing and implementing programs and services to meet the needs of clients
• 指导和协调资源配置 directing and coordinating the allocation of resources
• 同家长、董事会、资助机构、社区和教育机构讨论领域的合作与协调事宜 liaising with parents, boards, funding bodies, the community and educational institutions to discuss areas of cooperation and coordination
• 指导行政业务，如预算计划、准备报告、控制支出和招生 directing administrative operations such as budget planning, report preparation, monitoring and controlling expenditure, and student admissions
• 选拔、培训和管理工作人员 selecting, developing and managing the performance of staff
• 编写或安排其他人员编写报告、预算 preparing, or arranging for the preparation of, reports, budgets and forecasts
• 代表团队进行业务谈判，主持例会、研讨会、公共听证会和讨论会 representing the organisation in negotiations, and at conventions, seminars, public hearings and forums
