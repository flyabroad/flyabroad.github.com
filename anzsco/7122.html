ANZSCO 7122 钻井工、矿工和爆破工职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 装配、安放和操作钻探设备和矿业设备，通过引爆将物质与土壤分离。DRILLERS, MINERS AND SHOT FIRERS assemble, position and operate drilling rigs and mining plant, and detonate explosives to extract materials from the earth and demolish structures.

ANZSCO 7122 钻井工、矿工和爆破工技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有与下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF证书II 或 III级（ANZSCO技术4级） In Australia: AQF Certificate II or III (ANZSCO Skill Level 4)
• 在新西兰：NZ资格证2或3级（ANZSCO技术4级） In New Zealand: NZ Register Level 2 or 3 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 4)
• 至少一年的相关工作经验可以取代上述的资格证书。有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。At least one year of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed aboveIn some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification
• 可能需要注册或许可。Registration or licensing may be required.

ANZSCO 7122 钻井工、矿工和爆破工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 拆除、移动和组装钻探设备和附件。dismantling, moving and reassembling drilling rigs and accessory plant
• 对矿石、液体和气体进行取样并封装。taking samples of ore, liquids and gases and packaging them
• 负责轻微的保养和修理，为设备加润滑油并对其进行清理。performing minor maintenance and repairs, and lubricating and cleaning plant
• 记录井眼的性能细节和信息，详细记录操作面和地下开采工厂的操作。recording performance details and information obtained from wells, and keeping logs detailing operations operating surface and underground mining plant
• 开展发展工作，如打通新的竖井、传动、通风口、输送和控制室。undertaking development work such as opening up new shafts, drives, air vents, rises and crib rooms
• 定位钻孔的炸药，并使用雷管和弹药筒装置炸药。positioning explosives in bore holes and priming explosives using detonators and explosive cartridges
• 将线缆、保险丝和引爆线连接在弹药筒和雷管上，进行引爆。connecting wires, fuses and detonating cords to explosive cartridges and detonators, and detonating explosives
• 监控厂房的运转，确保其他工人在采矿点和钻孔期间的安全操作。monitoring operation of plant and ensuring safety of other workers on mining sites and during drilling operations
• 操作辅助设备，如用泵将空气、水和泥排除。operating auxiliary plant such as pumps to expel air, water and mud
