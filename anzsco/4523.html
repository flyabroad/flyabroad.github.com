ANZSCO 4523 体育教练、指导员和裁判主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 通过分析运动员的表现和发展能力对其进行训练、培训和指导。coaching, training and instructing sportspersons by analysing performances and developing abilities
• 规划和指导比赛策略，发展与运动模式并分析比赛进程。planning and directing game strategies, developing play patterns and analysing game progress
• 激励运动员和监督练习赛。motivating Sportspersons and supervising practice sessions
• 招聘区运动员和其他指导人员。recruiting players and other coaching staff
• 安排比赛入场。arranging entries into sporting competitions
• 宣传运动和技能发展，监督年轻运动员。promoting sports and skills development, and overseeing the participation of young people in sport
• 在比赛中担任裁判。officiating at sporting events to enforce rules
• 协调和指导比赛，联系其他官员解释和执行运动的规章和制度。coordinating and directing sporting activities, and liaising with other officials to interpret and enforce rules and regulations relating to sport
