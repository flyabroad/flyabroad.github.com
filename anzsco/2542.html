ANZSCO 2542 护理教育者和研究员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 护理教育者和研究员提供临床和理论指导，促进护士和助产士的专业发展，进行护理实践研究。NURSE EDUCATORS AND RESEARCHERS provide clinical and theoretical education to and promote professional development of nurses and midwives, and conduct research into nursing practice.

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平，且有5年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 Occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification and at least five years relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 2542 护理教育者和研究员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 研究、规划、发展和实施护理课程 researching, planning, developing and implementing nursing curricula
• 推进全科和专科护士的实践经历 facilitating practical experience for general and specialist nurses
• 评估当前持续变化的教育需求，监督课程开展情况 evaluating ongoing and changing educational needs and monitoring course outcomes
• 参与制定和实施影响护理、护士教育和健康的政策 participating in developing and implementing policies affecting nursing, nurse education and health
• 实施和促进护理学以及跨学科的研究项目，传播研究信息 undertaking and promoting nursing and interdisciplinary research projects, and disseminating research information
• 将当前研究结果应用到临床护理实践和病人管理上 promoting utilisation of current research findings into clinical nursing practice and patient management
• 促进安全、质量和风险管理的研究和研究成果的实施 promoting the implementation of research and research findings into organisation-wide functions such as safety, quality and risk management
• 为其他护士的研究提供支持和指导 providing support and education for other nurses undertaking research
