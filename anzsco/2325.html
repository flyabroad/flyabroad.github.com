ANZSCO 2325 室内设计师职业描述 - FLYabroad

• 室内设计师设计，绘制详图，监督商业，工业，零售和居住建筑的内部建设，通过强调空间制造，空间规划和提高生活和工作环境的因素，以达到与环境相和谐的目的。INTERIOR DESIGNERS plan, design, detail and supervise the construction of commercial, industrial, retail and residential building interiors to produce an environment tailored to a purpose, with particular emphasis on space creation, space planning, and factors that enhance living and working environments.

At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 2325 室内设计师主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 通过与客户和利益相关者进行咨询，来判定设计的目标和限制因素determining the objectives and constraints of the design brief by consulting with clients and stakeholders
• 研究和分析空间，功能，效率，安全和美学要求 researching and analysing spatial, functional, efficiency, safety and aesthetic requirements
• 使室内建筑设计理念规范化 formulating design concepts for building interiors
• 准备草图，图解，插图并计划交流设计理念preparing sketches, diagrams, illustrations and plans to communicate design concepts
• 与客户，管理者，供应商和建筑人员进行协商negotiating design solutions with clients, management, suppliers and construction staff
• 选择和推荐功能美学材料，家具和内部装修的产品selecting, specifying and recommending functional and aesthetic materials, furniture and products for interiors
• 对选择的设计方案详述并编档detailing and documenting selected design for construction
• 监督室内装饰supervising the construction of interiors
