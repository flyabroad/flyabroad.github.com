ANZSCO 31 工程、信息通信技术以及科学技师职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 工程，信息通信技术以及科学技师进行测试和实验，在农业、医学、科学、建筑、工程、信息通信技术和通信方面，向从事研究、设计和发展的专业人士提供技术支持。ENGINEERING, ICT AND SCIENCE TECHNICIANS perform tests and experiments, and provide technical support to Professionals engaged in research, design and development in the areas of agriculture, medicine, science, building, engineering, ICT and telecommunications.

ANZSCO 31 工程、信息通信技术以及科学技师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 进行科学测试、实验和计算，校对和分析结果 performing scientific tests, experiments and computations and collating and analysing the results
• 检测动物、植物、农产品和工具，确保在质量、卫生和许可方面符合政府和工业标准 inspecting animals, plants and agricultural produce to ensure compliance with government and industry standards
• 开发、操作和维护用于医疗诊断和实验研究的仪器和工具 developing, operating and maintaining instruments and equipment used in clinical diagnosis and experimental research
• 制作、解析、检查和修订图表、计划、表格、地图等 preparing, interpreting, inspecting and revising drawings, plans, diagrams, designs, maps and charts
• 安装、测试、修理和修改电器、电子设备和机械设备 installing, testing, repairing and modifying electrical, electronic and mechanical equipment
• 估算原材料成本和数量 estimating quantities and costs of materials
为远程通信网络、计算机硬件软件使用者提供技术支持 providing technical support for telecommunications networks, and to users of computer hardware and software
