• 制造和修理标准生产部件和用于特定用途的车体，安装、修理和更换车辆的内部修饰。VEHICLE BODY BUILDERS AND TRIMMERS manufacture and repair prototype production units and purpose-built vehicle bodies, and install, repair and replace the interior trim of vehicles

ANZSCO 3242 车身制造工和装饰工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 使用成型机、切割和焊接工具创造框架部分。constructing framework sections in metal, wood, fibreglass and other materials using shaping machines and cutting and welding equipment
• 将各部分进行螺栓连接、螺钉固定和焊接以形成完整的框架。bolting, screwing, riveting and welding sections together to form complete frameworks
• 对金属薄板、铝板和增强塑料板进行切割和成型加工，使用手动和电动工具讲起安放于框架内。cutting and shaping panels of sheetmetal, aluminium and reinforced plastic and attaching to frameworks using hand and power tools
• 修改装配线，以满足特定需求。modifying assembly line vehicles to special requirements
• 根据图纸和草图准备新的车辆修饰工作，去除旧的覆盖物和装置，并采取新的测量值。preparing new vehicle trim work according to drawings and sketches, and removing old coverings and fittings from vehicles and taking new measurements
• 选择并切割布料、人造革和皮革，使用重型缝纫机缝和。selecting and cutting pieces of fabric, vinyl and leather and sewing pieces together using heavy-duty sewing machines
• 安装内部的设计，如套管、地板和扶手。installing internal trim in vehicles such as lining, floor coverings and armrests
• 安装门饰板、橡皮垫、门锁和把手。attaching door trims, rubber seals, locks and handles
