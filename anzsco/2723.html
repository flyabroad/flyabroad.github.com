• 收集与客户相关的资料，评估他们的认知、行为和情绪障碍 collecting data about clients and assessing their cognitive, behavioural and emotional disorders
• 管理并解释诊断测试实验，并制定治疗方案 administering and interpreting diagnostic tests and formulating plans for treatment
• 个人和团体的治疗方案的开发、管理并评估 developing, administering and evaluating individual and group treatment programs
• 与其他专家协商治疗计划的细节 consulting with other professionals on details of cases and treatment plans
• 研究学习的积极性、团队合作以及个体在心智和教育行为上的差异 conducting research studies of motivation in learning, group performance and individual differences in mental abilities and educational performance

• 制定目标、诊断方法以及预测测试，以供教师计制定教学方法和教学内容 formulating achievement, diagnostic and predictive tests for use by teachers in planning methods and content of instruction

272311 临床心理学家 Clinical Psychologist

• 与个人和团体协商，评估心理障碍并执行治疗方案。需要注册和许可证。Consults with individuals and groups, assesses psychological disorders and administers programs of treatment. Registration or licensing is required.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1
• 职业方向： Specialisations:
• 犯罪心理学家 Forensic Psychologist
• 健康心理学家 Health Psychologist
• 神经心理学家 Neuropsychologist

272312 教育心理学家 Educational Psychologist

• 研究学习与教学，开发心理学技巧，以培养教育环境人员的发展和技能。需要注册和许可证。Investigates learning and teaching, and develops psychological techniques to foster the development and skills of individuals and groups in educational settings. Registration or licensing is required.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1

272313 组织心理学家 Organisational Psychologist

• 替代职位： Alternative Title:
• 工业心理学家 Industrial Psychologist
• 职业心理学家 Occupational Psychologist
• 应用心理学原理和技巧来研究职业行为，工作环境以及组织结构；解决工作业绩和组织设计方面的难题。需要注册和许可证。 Applies psychological principles and techniques to study occupational behaviour, working conditions and organisational structure, and solve problems of work performance and organisational design. Registration or licensing is required.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1

272314 精神治疗医师 Psychotherapist<br />
• 运用行为疗法、生物反馈、放松疗法等心理疗法，为精神和情绪障碍者提供诊断治疗。 Provides diagnosis and treatment of mental and emotional disorders using psychotherapeutic methods such as behavioural therapy, biofeedback, relaxation therapy and other techniques.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1
• 职业方向： Specialisations:
• 艺术精神治疗医师或治疗学家 Art Psychotherapist or Therapist
