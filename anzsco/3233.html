ANZSCO 3233 精密金属行业技工职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 精密金属行业工人制造，装配，维护并修理金属精密仪器。 SPRECISION METAL TRADES WORKERS fabricate, assemble, maintain and repair metal precision instruments.

ANZSCO 3233 精密金属行业技工技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有和下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF证书3级，包括至少2年的在职培训或AQF证书IV级（ANZSCO技术3级）In Australia: AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰：NNZ资格证4级（ANZSCO技术3级）In New Zealand: NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 至少3年的工作经验可以代替上述的资格证。At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above.
• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3233 精密金属行业技工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 组装精密工具、锁具、时钟和火器的部件与组件。 assembling parts and subassemblies of precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms
• 拆除精密仪器、锁、钟表和枪支；维修和更换残次部件；使用手动、电动工具和专门设计的机器来进行重组。 dismantling precision instruments, locks, timepieces and firearms, repairing and replacing defective parts, and reassembling articles using hand and power tools and specially designed machines
• 在珠宝、奖杯及其他饰品表面雕刻字母、数字和图案等。 inscribing letters, figures and designs on surfaces of jewellery, trophies and other ornamental items
• 安装安全系统，改变锁的换向齿轮，换锁，制作钥匙并开锁。 installing security systems, changing tumblers in locks, changing locks, cutting keys and opening locks by manipulation
• 用标准度量衡，夹具及固定装置，手动工具调整和排列部件和小的配重以达校准精密仪器的目的。 calibrating precision instruments using standard weights and measures, jigs and fixtures, and hand tools to adjust and align parts and small balancing weights
• 制造圆锯、带锯和其他动力锯的刀片，维修、设置和打磨刀片。 making blades for circular, band and other power saws and repairing, setting and sharpening blades for hand and power saws
• 测试电子时钟的电路。testing circuits in electronic timepieces
• 可能要估计维修费用并准备报价。 may estimate costs and prepare quotes for repairs
