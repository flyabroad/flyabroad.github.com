ANZSCO 8 体力劳动者职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 体力劳动者手工或使用电动工具、机械，可能个人单独或者协助团体中的技术工人，如技工、机械操作工和司机等，进行一些日常重复性的体力工作，LABOURERS perform a variety of routine and repetitive physical tasks using hand and power tools, and machines either as an individual or as part of a team assisting more skilled workers such as Trades Workers, and Machinery Operators and Drivers.

ANZSCO 8 体力劳动者技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 这一类别的大多数职业对学历和工作经验的要求如下： Most occupations in this major group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.
• 在澳大利亚： In Australia:
• AQF二级或三级证书，或1年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 4级水平）； AQF Certificate II or III, or at least one year of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 4); or
• 或AQF一级证书，或中等义务教育（ANZSCO 5级水平）。 AQF Certificate I, or compulsory secondary education (ANZSCO Skill Level 5)
• 在新西兰： In New Zealand:
• NZ二级或三级注册资格，或1年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 4级水平）； NZ Register Level 2 or 3 qualification, or at least one year of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 4); or
• 或NZ一级注册资格，或中等义务教育（ANZSCO 5级水平）。 NZ Register Level 1 qualification, or compulsory secondary education (ANZSCO Skill Level 5)
• 某些情况下，除了正式的学历要求，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训经历。对于技能水平为5级的职业，还需要一个短期的培训，可能是附加的也可能代替正规资格要求，或者不需要正规的资格和在职培训经验。 In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. In the case of some Skill Level 5 occupations, a short period of on-the-job training may be required in addition to or instead of the formal qualification, or no formal qualification or on-the-job training may be required.
