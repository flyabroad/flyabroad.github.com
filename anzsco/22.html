ANZSCO 22 商业、人力资源和市场营销专业人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 商业、人力资源和市场营销专业人员负责在财务核算、财务交易、人力资源开发、公共关系和市场营销方面提供一些分析性的、抽象的或实践性的建议，并进行经济、组织结构、调查方法和系统的研究。BUSINESS, HUMAN RESOURCE AND MARKETING PROFESSIONALS perform analytical, conceptual and practical tasks to provide services in financial accounting and transaction matters, human resource development, public relations and marketing, and conduct studies of the economy, organisational structures, methods and systems.

ANZSCO 22 商业、人力资源和市场营销专业人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 收集、分析和解释相关机构的财务可行性、成本结构和贸易可行性方面的信息 collecting, analysing and interpreting information on the financial viability, cost structures and trading effectiveness of organisations
• 进行财务审计，准备财务报表并为机构管理财务系统 conducting audits, preparing financial statements and controlling treasury systems for organisations
• 完善并审核财务计划和财务策略，制定买卖订单，并洽谈商品的购买和销售活动 developing and reviewing financial plans and strategies, executing buy and sell orders, and negotiating the purchase and sale of commodities
• 员工招聘、培训和发展计划的制定、实施和评估 developing, implementing and evaluating staff recruitment, training and development programs
• 研究、开发和实施市场营销和公关活动 researching, developing and implementing marketing and public relations campaigns
• 研究开发出改善和促进政府和企业经营能力和效率提高的方法策略 studying and developing methods and policies to improve and promote government and business operations and effectiveness
• 开发和管理能保存和销毁法律、行政、证据、历史和其他记录的档案系统 developing and managing record and archival systems for retention and destruction of legal, administrative, evidential, historical and other records
