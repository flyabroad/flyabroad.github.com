ANZSCO 1399 其他专业管理人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 本组包含其他未被分类的专业经理人，包含艺术类管理人、环境经理、实验室经理、质检经理、体育管理人。 This unit group covers Specialist Managers not elsewhere classified. It includes Arts Administrators or Managers, Environmental Managers, Laboratory Managers, Quality Assurance Managers and Sports Administrators.

139911 艺术类管理人 Arts Administrator or Manager

• 艺术类管理人负责企划、组织、指导、控制、协调和促进艺术和文化政策、计划、项目和服务。 Plans, organises, directs, controls, coordinates and promotes artistic and cultural policies, programs, projects and services.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1
• 职业方向： Specialisation:
• 美术馆主管 Art Gallery Director
• 社区艺术中心经理 Community Arts Centre Manager
• 文化中心经理 Cultural Centre Manager

139912 环境经理 Environmental Manager

• 负责企划、组织、指导、控制、协调环境管理体系的开发和实施，用来确认、解决、减轻环境问题如污染、废物处理等，过程中要遵守环境法规，确保企业可持续发展。Plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the development and implementation of an environmental management system within an organisation by identifying, solving and alleviating environmental issues, such as pollution and waste treatment, in compliance with environmental legislation and to ensure corporate sustainable development.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1

139913 实验室经理 Laboratory Manager

• 企划、组织、指导、控制、协调某个研究实验室或生产实验室的操作。Plans, organises, directs, controls and coordinates the operations of a research or production laboratory.
• 技能等级：1 Skill Level: 1

139915 体育管理人 Sports Administrator

• 企划、组织、指导、控制、协调和促进体育和娱乐活动，以及制定相关政策 Plans, organises, directs, controls, coordinates and promotes sport and recreational activities, and develops related policies.
