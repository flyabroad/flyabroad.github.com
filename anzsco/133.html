ANZSCO 133 建筑、分配和生产经理职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 建筑、分配和生产经理负责组织的建筑施工、工程设计、进口、出口和批发、制造业、生产、供给和分配活动的企划、组织、指导、控制和协调工作。 CONSTRUCTION, DISTRIBUTION AND PRODUCTION MANAGERS plan, organise, direct, control and coordinate building and construction, engineering, importing, exporting and wholesaling, manufacturing, production, supply and distribution activities within organisations.

ANZSCO 133 建筑、分配和生产经理技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验可以代替正规资格要求。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历。（ANZSCO 1级水平）Most occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 133 建筑、分配和生产经理主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 发现商机，制定实施商业计划，市场营销策略和运营方案，管理人力资源、产品定价、 信贷政策和程序。identifying business opportunities, and developing and implementing business plans and marketing, operating, human resource, pricing and credit policies and procedures
• 制定项目计划和预算 establishing project schedules and budgets
• 协调劳动力资源分配，物资、厂房设备的采购及运送 coordinating labour resources and procurement and delivery of materials, plant and equipment
• 与供应商和客户谈判协商以制定满足资源、花费和质量要求的方案 negotiating contracts with suppliers and customers to meet resource, cost and quality requirements
• 监控成本和工作进度，适时的调整工作进度和资源供给确保工作顺利完成 monitoring costs and work progress and performance, and adjusting processes and resources to ensure goals are met
• 指导编制生产记录和报告 directing the preparation of production records and reports
