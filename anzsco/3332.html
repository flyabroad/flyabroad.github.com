ANZSCO 3332 泥水匠职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 水泥匠安装和修理石膏板、天花板、消防系统、吸声瓦、复合墙体砖衬，并用石膏、水泥等装饰材料保护建筑物。PLASTERERS apply and fix plasterboard partitions, suspended ceilings, fire rating systems, acoustic tiles, and composite wall linings to buildings, and apply decorative and protective coverings of plaster, cement and similar materials to the interiors and exteriors of structures

ANZSCO 3332 泥水匠主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 确定石膏板布局，安装绝缘和隔气层。determining plasterboard layout, and installing insulation and vapour barriers
• 测量、标记和切割石膏板，提高和定位面板，并对其固定到墙上和天花板上。measuring, marking and cutting plasterboard, lifting and positioning panels, and securing them to walls, ceilings and battens
• 准备墙角护条并将其固定在适当的位置。preparing corner beads and securing them in position
• 固定预制檐口、平板模具、天花板中心。fixing pre-cast cornices, panel mouldings, ceiling centres and other plaster fittings
• 使用湿石膏和密封剂覆盖接口和钉子孔，并用刷子和砂纸进行打磨。 covering joins and nail holes with wet plaster and sealing compounds, and smoothing them using wet brushes and sand paper
• 用泥铲混合石膏、水泥和底灰，并将其涂在建筑物表层，校平并打磨以使厚度一致。mixing and applying coats of plaster, cement and render to structures using trowels, and levelling and smoothing coats to uniform thickness
• 铅测并校正转角、角度、墙壁和天花板。 plumbing and straightening corners, angles and wall and ceiling surfaces
• 在终饰层生成装饰纹理。 creating decorative textures in finishing coats
• 结合石膏、水泥等类似材料，涂抹并加工声学、绝缘以及防火材料 applying and finishing acoustic, insulating and fireproofing materials bonded with plaster, plastic cement and similar materials
