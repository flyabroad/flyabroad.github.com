• 协助机械工程研究，设计，制造，施工，运营，机械维护，生产设备，机械设施。 MECHANICAL ENGINEERING DRAFTSPERSONS AND TECHNICIANS assist in mechanical engineering research, design, manufacture, construction, operation and maintenance of machines, manufacturing equipment, mechanical installations and facilities.

ANZSCO 3125 机械工程起草者和技术人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 在机械工程师和工程技术人员的指导下，为机械工程工作准备计划草案和设计。preparing drawings, plans and designs for mechanical engineering work under the direction of Mechanical Engineers and Engineering Technologists
• 协助机械工程师和技术人员设计机械设备 assisting Mechanical Engineers and Engineering Technologists in the design of mechanical equipment and plant
• 选择工具和设备 selecting tools and equipment
• 组装和安装新修改的机械装置，零部件，机床，控制设备，液压动力系统 assembling and installing new and modified mechanical assemblies, components, machine tools and controls, and hydraulic power systems
• 估计材料成本，数量和机器要求 estimating material costs and quantities, and machine requirements
• 执行和指挥现场和实验室测试 performing and directing field and laboratory tests
• 手机和分析数据，进行复杂的计算和准备图 collecting and analysing data, carrying out complex computations and preparing diagrams
• 组织和监督检查并维修机器和工厂 organising and supervising inspection and maintenance of machines and plant
• 确保设计和完成的工作符合规范，法规以及合同规定 ensuring that designs and finished work are within specifications, regulations and contract provisions
