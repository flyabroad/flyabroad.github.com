ANZSCO 2326 城市及区域规划师主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 搜集并分析影响土地使用的数据，如：经济、法律、政治、文化、人口、社会及自然环境等因素 compiling and analysing data on economic, legal, political, cultural, demographic, sociological, physical and environmental factors affecting land use
• 与政府部门、社区、建筑师、法律职业人员、规划师及环境专业人员沟通协商 conferring with government authorities, communities, Architects, social scientists, Legal Professionals, and planning, development and environmental specialists
• 设计并推荐土地的开发和使用，向组织和个体展示情节设计、平面规划方案 devising and recommending use and development of land, and presenting narrative and graphic plans, programs and designs to groups and individuals
• 建议政府和组织机构进行城市区域规划和资源规划 advising governments and organisations on urban and regional planning and resource planning
• 评审环境影响报告 reviewing and evaluating environmental impact reports
• 遵守建筑法规和土地分区法规及其他法律事宜 staying up-to-date with changes in building and zoning codes, regulations and other legal issues
• 调解有关规划建议和规划项目的纠纷 may serve as mediators in disputes over planning proposals and projects
• 向政府部门推荐规划建议 may speak at public meetings and appear before government to explain planning proposals
