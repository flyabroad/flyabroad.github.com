ANZSCO 252 理疗保健专业人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 理疗保健专业人员评估、诊断和治疗疾病，提供治疗服务，例如脊椎按摩疗法、整骨疗法、健康补充疗法、牙科、职业疗法、物理疗法、足部疗法、言语病理学和听力学疗法。营养师、医学影像专业人员、劳动卫生与环境卫生专业人员、验光师和视力矫正师、药剂师、健康促进官员、义肢矫形师或修复学家不包括在本小组中。他们在251健康诊断和促进专家那组。 HEALTH THERAPY PROFESSIONALS assess, diagnose and treat illnesses and disabilities, and provide therapeutic services such as chiropractic, osteopathy, complementary health, dentistry, occupational therapy, physical therapy, podiatry, speech pathology and audiology. Dietitians, Medical Imaging Professionals, Occupational and Environmental Health Professionals, Optometrists and Orthoptists, Pharmacists, Health Promotion Officers, and Orthotists or Prosthetists are excluded from this minor group. They are included in Minor Group 251 Health Diagnostic and Promotion Professionals.

ANZSCO 252 理疗保健专业人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 In Australia and New Zealand: Occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 252 理疗保健专业人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 询问、检查、观察和测试病人，确定病因 questioning, examining, observing and testing patients to identify and determine nature of disease, disorder, illness or problem
• 根据病人的病情设计、制定和实施治疗方案 designing, developing and implementing treatment plans to address patients' problems
• 提出运动、饮食、生活方式、卫生准则和建议，提供合适的仪器和矫正方法 providing exercise, dietary, lifestyle and hygiene guidelines and advice, adaptive equipment and correctional aids
• 记录病人病史，例如之前的损伤、手术、总体的健康状况和生活方式 recording patients' medical histories such as previous injuries, surgeries, general health and lifestyle
• 评估和记录病人的治疗反应和进展 evaluating and documenting patients' treatment response and progress
