• 教授必修课程 delivering prescribed courses of study
• 监督并评估学生的进步 monitoring and assessing the progress of individual students
• 致力于学生的社交、情绪、智力及体质健康，并维护可接受的行为规范 attending to the social, emotional, intellectual and physical wellbeing of students and maintaining acceptable standards of conduct
• 与其他专业人员、家长以及学生讨论个人进步问题、课程、学术研究等 discussing individual progress, courses, academic matters and problems with other professionals, parents and students

• 开展理论与实践方面的专题讲座、辅导及研讨会 preparing and presenting theoretical and practical subject matter in lectures, tutorials and workshop sessions
• 对特定领域的教育进程、课程设计等进行研究并提出建议 conducting research and advising on particular areas of the education process, curriculum coordination and design
