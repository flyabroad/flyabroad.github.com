ANZSCO 3993 画廊、博物馆或图书馆技术员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 准备用于收藏的艺术品、样品和人工制品，安排和组织展览，协助管理员组织和操作用于处理记载材料和文档的系统。GALLERY, LIBRARY AND MUSEUM TECHNICIANS prepare artworks, specimens and artefacts for collections, arrange and construct gallery exhibits,and assist Librarians to organise and operate systems for handling recorded material and files.

ANZSCO 3993 画廊、博物馆或图书馆技术员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有与下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF大专文凭、高级文凭或文凭（ANZSCO技术2级）In Australia: AQF Associate Degree, Advanced Diploma or Diploma (ANZSCO Skill Level 2)
• 在新西兰：NZ登记证书（ANZSCO技术2级）In New Zealand:NZ Register Diploma (ANZSCO Skill Level 2)
• 至少3年的工作经验可以代替上述的资格证。At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above.
• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3993 画廊、博物馆或图书馆技术员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 准备用于展览的物品。 mounting and preparing objects for display
• 设计和安排室内陈设、展柜和展区。designing and arranging exhibit furnishings, display cases and display areas
• 协助安装灯光和显示设备。assisting in setting up lighting and display equipment
• 接受、运送、包装和取出展品。receiving, shipping, packing and unpacking exhibits
• 订购新藏件，维护图书馆记录和流通系统。ordering new library materials and maintaining library records and circulation systems
• 对印刷和记录的材料按目录分类。cataloguing printed and recorded material
• 将数据录入数据库并编辑记录。entering data into databases and editing computer records
• 操作音像和复印设备。operating audiovisual and reprographic equipment
• 搜索和确认著录资料。searching and verifying bibliographic data
