ANZSCO 3511 面包师和面点师职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 准备和烘培面包、面包卷、圆甜饼、蛋糕、点心和其它西点。BAKERS AND PASTRYCOOKS prepare and bake bread loaves and rolls, buns, cakes, biscuits and pastry goods

• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3511 面包师和面点师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 生产前，检查设备的清洁度和运转情况，以确保遵守职业卫生和安全守则。checking the cleanliness of equipment and operation of premises before production runs to ensure compliance with occupational health and safety regulations
• 检查原材料质量，并对材料称重。checking the quality of raw materials and weighing ingredients
• 揉面、发酵成熟、切割、模制、混合和塑型。kneading, maturing, cutting, moulding, mixing and shaping dough and pastry goods
• 准备填料。preparing pastry fillings
• 监控烤箱温度和产品外观，以确定烘烤时间。monitoring oven temperatures and product appearance to determine baking times
• 进行面包等西点的塑性、装载、烘焙、卸载、脱模和冷藏。coordinating the forming, loading, baking, unloading, de-panning and cooling of batches of bread, rolls and pastry products
• 为面包和甜点制作糖皮，使用奶油和脆皮装饰蛋糕。glazing buns and pastries, and decorating cakes with cream and icing
• 操作面粉转动和发酵机以及面包切割机。operating machines which roll and mould dough and cut biscuits
• 清空、清洗烤盘、烤模和其它烹饪设备，并用油脂润滑。emptying, cleaning and greasing baking trays, tins and other cooking equipment
