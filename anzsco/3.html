ANZSCO 3 工程、ICT和科学技术人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 工程、ICT和科学技术人员进行测试和实验，为从事于农业、医药、科学、建筑、工程、ICT和电信行业的研究、设计和开发的专业人员，提供技术支持。 ENGINEERING, ICT AND SCIENCE TECHNICIANS perform tests and experiments, and provide technical support to Professionals engaged in research, design and development in the areas of agriculture, medicine, science, building, engineering, ICT and telecommunications.

ANZSCO 3 工程、ICT和科学技术人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 执行测试和实验，并为健康专业人员、自然和物理科学专业人员和工程技术人员提供技术支持。 carrying out tests and experiments, and providing technical support to Health Professionals, Natural and Physical Science Professionals and Engineering Professionals
• 为计算机软硬件用户提供技术支持。 providing technical support to users of computer hardware and software
• 装配、修理和保养铁质、木制、玻璃和纺织产品。 fabricating, repairing and maintaining metal, wood, glass and textile products
• 修理和保养机动车辆、飞机、船舶以及电气、电子产品和设备。repairing and maintaining motor vehicles, aircraft, marine craft and electrical and electronic machines and equipment
• 构造、修理、装修建筑物等。constructing, repairing, fitting-out and finishing buildings and other structures
• 操作印刷和装订设备。 operating printing and binding equipment
• 准备并烹饪食物。 preparing and cooking food
• 为动物剪毛，照顾、培训、喂养动物，并协助兽医。shearing, caring for, training and grooming animals, and assisting Veterinarians
• 繁殖及栽培植物，为体育赛事铺设和维护草皮。 propagating and cultivating plants, and establishing and maintaining turf surfaces for sporting events
• 理发、造型。 cutting and styling hair
• 操作化学、天然气、石油和电力设备。 operating chemical, gas, petroleum and power generation equipment
• 为文艺演出的制片、录音和广播提供技术帮助。 providing technical assistance for the production, recording and broadcasting of artistic performances
