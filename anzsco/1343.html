ANZSCO 1343 校长职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 校长负责企划、组织、指导、控制和协调小学、中学、高中阶段的教育、行政方面事宜（包括物质人力资源方面）。 SCHOOL PRINCIPALS plan, organise, direct, control and coordinate the educational and administrative aspects of primary, middle or intermediate, and secondary schools including physical and human resources.

ANZSCO 1343 校长技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本类大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验（ANZSCO 1级水平）。需要注册和许可证。
In Australia and New Zealand: Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification and at least five years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).Registration or licensing is required.

ANZSCO 1343 校长主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 根据教育主管部门设置的课程确定教育规划 determining educational programs based on curricula set by educational authorities
• 通过管理系统监控学校业绩、学生入学情况 implementing systems and procedures to monitor school performance and student enrolments
• 学生入学和教育服务的管理与行政工作 directing administrative and clerical activities concerning student admissions and educational services
• 编制并控制学校预算 preparing school budgets and maintaining budgetary controls
• 促进教育计划，并代表学校出席各种场合 promoting the educational program, and representing schools in the wider community
• 管理学校设施 supervising the maintenance of school facilities
• 强化纪律守则,为师生提供安全、舒适的环境 enforcing a disciplinary code to create a safe and conducive environment for students and teachers
• 与家长一起筹集更多的基金 organising and implementing methods of raising additional funds in conjunction with parent groups
• 选拔、培训和管理员工 controlling selection, training and supervision of staff
• 讲课 may teach students
