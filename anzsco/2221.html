ANZSCO 2221 金融证券师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 监测影响商品供需的商品价格，趋势和其他因素 monitoring commodity prices, trends and other factors affecting the supply and demand for commodities
• 协商商品的购买和销售，如谷物，羊毛，矿物和金属产品 negotiating the purchase and sale of commodities such as grains, wool, minerals and metals
• 决定客户特殊的金融和保险需求，调查和审查可行的金融和保险产品以满足客户需求 determining the specific financial and insurance requirements of clients, and researching and reviewing available finance and insurance products for suitability to meet clients' requirements
• 分析客户的财务状况，讨论金融期权并制定财务政策 analysing clients' financial status, discussing financial options and developing financial strategies
• 推荐满足客户需求的贷款组合 recommending loan combinations that meet clients' needs
• 与潜在的客户面谈以说明保险政策条件，保险范围，保险费率和收益，对保险数额和范围进行建议 interviewing prospective clients to explain insurance policy conditions, risks covered, premium rates and benefits, and to make recommendations on the amount and type of cover
• 通过银行，贷款人，融资人和保险公司为客户准备保险，住房代理抵押和其他金融产品 arranging insurance, home loan mortgages and other types of finance for clients through banks, lenders, financiers and insurance companies
• 准备能够体现金融条件，还款和贷款日期的相关文件 preparing documents which set out the conditions of finance, repayments and loan periods
• 对客户保险重要的风险变化进行鉴别和建议 identifying and advising on significant risk changes to clients' insurance
• 代理综合和商务租赁，设备融资，商业融资，项目融资以及资产开发金融 broking complex and commercial leases, equipment finance, commercial finance, project finance and finance for property developers
