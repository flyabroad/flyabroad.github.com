ANZSCO 272 社会和福利专业人士职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 社会和福利专业人士为客户或社团提供社会、教育、职业、关系及精神方面的指导，使他们能够克服困难、寻找并利用资源达到特殊目标；从现代和历史的角度研究人类行为、社会体系。 SOCIAL AND WELFARE PROFESSIONALS provide guidance to clients and the community in social, educational, vocational, relationship and spiritual matters to enable them to overcome difficulties and to find and use resources to achieve particular goals; and study human behaviour, society and institutions from current and historical perspectives.

ANZSCO 272 社会和福利专业人士技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验可以代替正规资格要求。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 Most occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 272 社会和福利专业人士主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 对健康、福利、休闲、住房供给、就业和其他社区服务资源予以评估 assessing resources for health, welfare, recreation, housing, employment and other community services
• 为正在经历婚姻问题、失业、疾病困扰或有毒瘾的客户提供支持并探索可行方案 providing support while exploring alternatives with clients who experience difficulties such as marital problems, unemployment, illness and drug abuse
• 与客户协商制定康复计划，考虑到职业、社会、医疗和心理需求 consulting with clients to develop rehabilitation plans taking account of vocational, social, medical and psychological needs
• 协助客户的就业安置以及涉及到教育培训的其他事宜 assisting clients with employment placement and matters relating to education and training
• 开发、管理并评估个人和团体的待遇问题 developing, administering and evaluating individual and group treatment programs
• 根据公认的仪式要求和信仰，主持公共礼拜；根据习俗、教会法及民法主持结婚、葬礼和特殊追思礼仪 preparing and conducting services of public worship and acknowledgments of faith, and performing marriages, funerals and special memorial services according to tradition and ecclesiastical and civil law
• 研究人类活动的历史和文化，报告研究结果 undertaking historical and cultural research into human activity, and preparing reports of research findings
