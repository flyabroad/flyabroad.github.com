ANZSCO 2726 福利、娱乐和社区艺术工作者职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 福利、娱乐和社区艺术工作者设计并实施政策与计划以满足社区和个人的要求；协助个人，家庭以及团体处理社会、情感和经济困难，通过教育支持、改变生活环境改善他们的生活质量。 WELFARE, RECREATION AND COMMUNITY ARTS WORKERS design and implement strategies and programs to meet community and individual needs and assist individuals, families and groups with social, emotional and financial difficulties to improve quality of life by educating and supporting them and working towards change in their social environment.

ANZSCO 2726 福利、娱乐和社区艺术工作者技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验可以代替正规资格要求。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 2726 福利、娱乐和社区艺术工作者主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 研究分析社区争点、需求和问题 researching and analysing community issues, needs and problems
• 开发、评估并维护社区资源和计划 developing, evaluating and maintaining community resources and programs
• 评估资料、撰写报告，比如为现有项目和新项目的资金需求撰写意见书 evaluating data and writing reports such as submissions requesting funding for continuing programs and new projects
• 通过社区调查、 识别地方的需求、关注点和愿望 identifying issues of local need, concerns and aspirations through community consultation
• 组织地方的体育、文化和娱乐活动，诸如社区活动、兴趣课、社区艺术项目以及体育竞赛等 organising local sporting, cultural and recreational events and activities such as community functions, hobby classes, community arts projects and sporting competitions
• 为正在经历婚姻问题、失业、疾病困扰或有毒瘾的客户提供支持并探索可行方案 providing support while exploring alternatives with clients who experience difficulties such as marital problems, unemployment, illness and drug abuse
• 为家庭暴力、虐待儿童、灾难以及其他危机的受害者，提供集中的短期心理辅导 assessing risks and providing intensive short-term crisis counselling for victims of domestic violence, child abuse, disasters and other crises
• 协助建立并管理邻里关系、社区团体、就业培训计划以及其他服务 assisting to establish and administer neighbourhood houses, community groups, employment training programs and other services
