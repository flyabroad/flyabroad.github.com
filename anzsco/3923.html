ANZSCO 3923 印刷工职责概述 - FLYabroad

设置和操作凸版、平版、柔版、凹版、报纸、既时、数字和胶版印刷机。PRINTERS set up and operate letterpress, lithographic, flexographic, gravure, newspaper, instant, digital and offset printing presses.

ANZSCO 3923 印刷工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 安装、调整和监控衬底馈电装置、输送装置、墨印系统和其它打印功能。setting, adjusting and monitoring substrate-feed mechanisms, delivery mechanisms, inking systems and other printing machine functions
• 混和油墨和溶剂至标准值，打印期间调节纸张和油墨的供应量。mixing ink and solvents to standard, and regulating paper and ink supply during print runs
• 监控、评估和确定打印机运转情况，以此来检查印刷质量和故障。monitoring, evaluating and determining press operations manually and by computer to check print quality standards against proofs and detect malfunctions
• 使用凸版、平版印、柔版和凹版印刷机（内嵌精整系统）产生一系列的印刷品。producing a variety of printed products using relief, lithographic, flexographic and gravure printing presses, and in-line finishing systems
• 为小型平版印刷机准备平板、橡皮布和牙印滚筒。preparing plates, blankets and impression cylinders on small offset lithographic printing presses
• 将纸放入送纸机构中。loading paper into feeding mechanisms
• 监控机器运转情况和印刷质量。monitoring machine operations and quality of printing
• 负责机器的维护、调整、修理和清洗。undertaking maintenance, adjustment, repair and cleaning of machines
• 产生和管理数印图像，并传送和输出。producing and managing digital print images, and transferring and outputting images
• 可能要设置和操作纸张和装订切纸。may set up and operate paper and bookbinding guillotines
