ANZSCO 2 专业人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 专业人员通过对理论知识的应用以及艺术、媒体、商业、设计、工程、物理和生命科学、运输、教育、健康、信息通讯技术、法律、社会科学和社会福利等领域的工作经验从事分析的、抽象的和创作性的工作。 PROFESSIONALS perform analytical, conceptual and creative tasks through the application of theoretical knowledge and experience in the fields of the arts, media, business, design, engineering, the physical and life sciences, transport, education, health, information and communication technology, the law, social sciences and social welfare.

ANZSCO 2 专业人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 这一类别的大多数职业对学历和工作经验的要求如下： Most occupations in this major group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.
• 在澳大利亚： In Australia:
• 本科或更高学历。五年以上相关经验可以取代正式的学历要求（ANZSCO 1级水平）； Bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1); or
• 或AQF大专学历、高级文凭或毕业证书，或至少3年的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）；AQF Associate Degree, Advanced Diploma or Diploma, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2)
• 在新西兰： In New Zealand:
• 本科或更高学历。五年以上相关经验可以取代对正式学历的要求（ANZSCO 1级水平）；Bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1); or
• 或新西兰注册文凭，或3年以上的相关工作经验（ANZSCO 2级水平）。 NZ Register Diploma, or at least three years of relevant experience (ANZSCO Skill Level 2).
• 有些情况下，除了正式的学历要求，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训经历。 In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification.
• 部分职业，如在21类别 艺术和媒体专业人员，需要很高的创作才华、个人对工作的执着和兴趣，或有任职资格或经验。Some occupations, such as those in Sub-Major Group 21 Arts and Media Professionals, require high levels of creative talent or personal commitment and interest as well as, or in place of, formal qualifications or experience.

ANZSCO 2 专业人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 通过语言、印刷品、电子媒体和艺术媒体包括视觉和表演艺术传达思想理念。communicating ideas through language, printed and electronic media, and artistic media including the visual and performing arts
• 分析、计划、发展和实施项目计划以及解决商业和经济问题。 analysing, planning, developing and implementing programs and solutions to resolve business and economic problems;

• 指导和分析研究项目以拓展科学领域的知识并运用这些知识发展技术。conducting and analysing research to extend the body of knowledge in the field of the sciences and developing techniques to apply this knowledge
• 设计商品、建筑以及其他物质构筑物和工程系统。 designing products, buildings and other physical structures, and engineering systems；
• 研究和发展课程体系，并在教育机构教授学生。researching and developing curricula, and teaching students in a range of educational settings；
• 设计、实施、测试和维护能够使使用、存储、控制、处理和散播信息的技术和服务。 designing, implementing, testing and maintaining technologies and services that enable information to be accessed, stored, manipulated, processed, and disseminated
• 对健康、社会和个人问题进行识别、处理和建议。 identifying, treating, and advising on, health, social, and personal issues
• 为客户提供法律方面的建议。advising clients on legal matters
