ANZSCO 2113 摄影师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 与客户协商确定照片的目的和用途 consulting with clients to determine objectives of photographic assignments
• 选择相机、胶片、滤光器和照明设备的种类并学习他们的使用要求 studying requirements of assignment and selecting type of camera, film, filter and lighting
• 将设备从工作室运到指定地点，并负责安装 working from a studio, and transporting and setting up equipment at assigned locations
• 测试光照水平并决定曝光度 measuring light levels and determining exposure
• 调整拍摄角度和光圈设置以达到预期成像 adjusting camera angles, aperture settings and subjects to achieve desired composition
• 调整灯光和滤光器以突出色彩亮点 adjusting lighting and filters to accentuate highlights and colours
• 调制摄影化学品，处理胶卷并冲洗照片 mixing photographic chemicals, processing film and developing prints
• 用数字成像技术和新颖的暗房技术处理图像，如调整曝光和显影时间 manipulating images using digital imaging techniques and creative darkroom techniques such as adjusting exposure and development times
• 可能会制作放大照片和有框照片 may make enlarged prints, and mount and frame prints
