ANZSCO 2332 土木工程专业人员职业描述 - FLYabroad

• 土木工程专业人员设计，组织和监督诸如水坝，桥梁，管道，煤气和供水计划时，排污系统，公路，机场和其他结构的土木工程项目建设；分析土壤和岩石在放置按照拟议的结构和设计压力结构基础；分析所有类型的结构静力性能和试验的行为和他们的建筑用材料的耐久性；规划和发展交通运输系统，以及估算和监测项目的建设成本。 CIVIL ENGINEERING PROFESSIONALS design, plan, organise and oversee the construction of civil engineering projects such as dams, bridges, pipelines, gas and water supply schemes, sewerage systems, roads, airports and other structures; analyse the likely behaviour of soil and rock when placed under pressure by proposed structures and design structural foundations; analyse the statical properties of all types of structures and test the behaviour and durability of materials used in their construction; plan and develop transportation systems; and estimate and monitor the construction costs of projects.

ANZSCO 2332 土木工程专业人员主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 确定施工方法，材料和质量标准，起草并理解技术规范,图纸，计划，建筑方法和程序 determining construction methods, materials and quality standards, and drafting and interpreting specifications, drawings, plans, construction methods and procedures
• 组织和指导现场劳动并交付建筑材料机械和设备，确立详细的协调现场活动的计划 organising and directing site labour and the delivery of construction materials, plant and equipment, and establishing detailed programs for the coordination of site activities
• 获得不同地点不同深度的土地和岩石样本，测试样本以决定在施工时对土地和岩石构成影响的力量，压缩性等因素，并决定土壤的安全负荷 obtaining soil and rock samples at different depths across sites and testing samples to determine strength, compressibility and other factors that affect the behaviour of soil and rock when a structure is imposed and determining the safe loading for the soil
• 研究建筑工程图纸来评估总成本，准备详细的成本计划，并把评估作为协助手段来进行预算控制 studying architectural and engineering drawings and specifications to estimate total costs, and preparing detailed cost plans and estimates as tools to assist in budgetary control
• 审查设计变化，评估对成本的影响，测定、评价、协商设计的变化 monitoring changes to designs, assessing effects on cost, and measuring, valuing and negotiating variations to designs
• 为静态和动态的载荷分析结构系统 analysing structural systems for both static and dynamic loads
• 设计结构以确保建筑不会发生坍塌，弯折或摇摆 designing structures to ensure they do not collapse, bend, twist or vibrate in undesirable ways
• 评估目前和未来的人口流动模式，要将人口增加和需求变化考虑在内 assessing present and future travel flow patterns taking into account population increase and needs change
• 设计运输系统的实际面貌，例如高速公路，铁路，城市公共交通，航空运输，后勤供应系统和终端 designing the physical aspects of transportation systems such as highways, railroads, urban transit, air transportation, logistical supply systems and their terminals
