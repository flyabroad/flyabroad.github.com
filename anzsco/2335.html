ANZSCO 2335 工业工程师，机械和生产工程师职业描述 - FLYabroad

• 工业工程师，机械和生产工程师主要负责设计，组织和统筹机械和加工厂房和设备的建造，运行和维护，确定生产活动的协调程序并确保资源利用的成本有效性。INDUSTRIAL, MECHANICAL AND PRODUCTION ENGINEERS design, organise and oversee the construction, operation and maintenance of mechanical and process plant and installations, establish programs for the coordination of manufacturing activities, and ensure usage of resources is cost effective.

ANZSCO 2335 工业工程师，机械和生产工程师主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 研究功能说明，组织结构图和项目信息，以决定工作人员和单位的具体作用和责任并确定可以使用的领域 studying functional statements, organisational charts and project information to determine functions and responsibilities of workers and work units and to identify areas of duplication
• 确定工作测量程序并分析工作样本，完善劳动力使用规范 establishing work measurement programs and analysing work samples to develop standards for labour utilisation
• 分析劳动力使用，设备布局，运行数据和生产进度及成本，使工人和设备的效率最大化 analysing workforce utilisation, facility layout, operational data and production schedules and costs to determine optimum worker and equipment efficiencies
• 设计制造业机械设备，机器，组件，产品以及建造的厂房和系统 designing mechanical equipment, machines, components, products for manufacture, and plant and systems for construction
• 完善制造标准并决定材料，设备，管道，物料流以及厂房和系统的容量和布局 developing specifications for manufacture, and determining materials, equipment, piping, material flows, capacities and layout of plant and systems
• 组织和管理项目劳动力以及材料，厂房和设备的交付 organising and managing project labour and the delivery of materials, plant and equipment
• 根据工程学原理和安全规章，确立安装，调试，质量控制，测试，审查和维护的标准和政策 establishing standards and policies for installation, modification, quality control, testing, inspection and maintenance according to engineering principles and safety regulations
• 视察工厂，确保其性能得到最大的维护 inspecting plant to ensure optimum performance is maintained
• 指导工厂建筑和设备的维护，协调新的设计，调查和维修进度 directing the maintenance of plant buildings and equipment, and coordinating the requirements for new designs, surveys and maintenance schedules
