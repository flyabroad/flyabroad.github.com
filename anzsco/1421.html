• 确定产品组合、库存水平和服务标准 determining product mix, stock levels and service standards
• 制定和执行采购营销策略，定价 formulating and implementing purchasing and marketing policies, and setting prices
• 为公司产品做促销，和广告 promoting and advertising the establishment‘s goods and services
• 向顾客销售产品和服务，并在产品使用方面提供建议 selling goods and services to customers and advising them on product use
• 维护库存水平和财务往来记录 maintaining records of stock levels and financial transactions
• 为公司做财务预算 undertaking budgeting for the establishment
• 员工的选拔、培训和管理 controlling selection, training and supervision of staff
• 遵守职业健康和安全条例 ensuring compliance with occupational health and safety regulations
