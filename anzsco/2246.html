ANZSCO 2246 图书管理员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 图书管理员主要负责改进，组织和管理图书馆服务，如信息收集，娱乐资源和阅览者信息服务。图书管理教师不在此类职业范围之内，而属于241 学校教师类别。 LIBRARIANS develop, organise and manage library services such as collections of information, recreational resources and reader information services. Teacher-Librarians are excluded from this unit group. Teacher-Librarians are included in Minor Group 241 School Teachers.

ANZSCO 2246 图书管理员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 改进和实施图书馆和信息的政策及服务 developing and implementing library and information policies and services
• 检阅出版物和材料，与出版商代表进行面谈，并与其他人协商以挑选图书馆的材料 examining publications and materials, interviewing publishers' representatives, and consulting with others to select library materials
• 针对读者的需求对服务进行检验，评估和调整 reviewing, evaluating and modifying services in response to user needs
• 协助访问图书馆的顾客获得需要的资料来源 providing assistance to clients in accessing library resources
• 管理图书馆系统以记录和组织图书馆藏书，收购和购买的图书，登记书籍的出借，并负责编排目录索引，进行文件归档和检索 managing library systems for recording and organising library holdings, acquisitions and purchases, reader registrations and loan transactions, and supervising indexing, filing and retrieval activities
• 为顾客进行信息检索 undertaking information research activities on behalf of clients
• 对图书馆和信息资源进行挑选，订购，分类和编目 selecting, ordering, classifying and cataloguing library and information resources
• 对藏书的完善和挑选进行监督 monitoring collection development and culling programs
• 管理和培训其他的图书馆工作人员 supervising and training other library staff
• 可能会对图书馆服务的改善和扩展活动进行计划和指导 may plan and direct library promotion and outreach activities
