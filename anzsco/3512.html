ANZSCO 3513 屠夫和肉类加工者主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 备肉标准：剔骨、去肥肉并进行切割、剁碎和研磨。preparing meat for sale by removing bones, trimming fat and cutting, mincing and grinding meat to shape and size for display or as ordered

• 选肉和备肉。selecting and preparing meat to produce smallgoods
• 使用机器进行如下处理：研磨、混合、切碎和变嫩。operating machines to grind, mix, mince and tenderise meat
• 使用混合香料、盐和其它作料来制作调料和咸菜。making seasonings and pickles by mixing spices, salt and other ingredients
• 操作香肠充填机、薰房、炒锅和蒸煮锅。operating sausage filling machines, smoking chambers, and cooking kettles and vats
• 对肉片的使用提出建议。advising customers on the suitability and uses of cuts of meat
• 可能要协助制定菜单和时间表，并估计生产成本。may assist in menu planning and scheduling, and in estimating food production costs
