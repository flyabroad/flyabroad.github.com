ANZSCO 394 木材行业工作者职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 木材行业工作者制造、修理并装饰木制家具，将木材零件组装成家具，操作木工机床和木材车削处理木材，制成相框等其他商品。WOOD TRADES WORKERS fabricate, repair and finish wooden furniture and fit and assemble prepared wooden parts to make furniture, set up and operate woodworking machines and wood turning lathes to shape wood stock, and make picture frames and other wood products.

ANZSCO 394 木材行业工作者技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有与下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF证书3级，包括至少2年的在职培训或AQF证书IV级（ANZSCO技术级别 3）In Australia: AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰：NNZ资格证4级（ANZSCO技术等级 3）In New Zealand: NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 至少3年的工作经验可以代替上述的资格证。At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above.
• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 394 木材行业行业工作者主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 检查图纸、定单和样品以确定参数。examining drawings, work orders and sample parts to determine specifications
• 选择和使用材料，如木料、薄木片、碎料板和合成木材。selecting and working with materials such as timber, veneers, particle board and synthetic wood
• 使用工具切削和塑造木材。marking out, cutting and shaping wood using tools ranging from hand tools to large timber cutting machines
• 确定工具和机器要求。determining tooling and machine requirements
• 安装各部件。assembling parts to form sections of furniture and completed articles
• 修理和完成家具的制作repairing and finishing furniture
• 安装铰链、锁、窗钩、橱柜和架子。fitting hinges, locks, catches, drawers and shelves
• 制作相框和其它木制品。making picture frames and other wood products.
