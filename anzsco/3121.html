ANZSCO 3121 建筑建设及土地测量人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 通过对建筑选址，建筑时间预估，费用预算，卫生管道的监督和检测，收集和评调查数据，准备地图和规划，为建设管理者，建筑师提供技术支持ARCHITECTURAL, BUILDING AND SURVEYING TECHNICIANS perform technical functions to assist Construction Managers, Architects and Surveyors by supervising and inspecting construction sites, estimating time, costs and resources, inspecting plumbing work, and collecting and evaluating survey data and preparing maps and plans.

ANZSCO 3121 建筑建设及土地测量人员职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 协助建设经理，建筑师和测量师进行规划和组织 assisting Construction Managers, Architects and Surveyors in planning and organisation
• 阐释规划，法律法规和行为守则 interpreting plans, regulations and codes of practice
• 准备设计原始草图，施工图和技术规范 preparing preliminary sketches, working drawings and specifications
• 整理，编辑和修改计划，地图和图表 preparing, editing and revising plans, maps, charts and drawings
• 协调工程进度安排 coordinating works programs
• 监督建筑进程和建筑材料以使其符合技术规范和法律法规要求 inspecting work and materials for compliance with specifications, regulations and standards
• 计算开支和估算时间进度 calculating costs and estimating time scales
• 使用测绘仪和摄影测量仪器收集相关数据 collecting data using surveying instruments and photogrammetric equipment
• 进行日常计算并绘制初始数据 performing routine computations and plotting preliminary data
