ANZSCO 2721 顾问 Counsellors - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 2721 顾问职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 顾问提供涉及到职业、关系、社会和教育方面的困难和问题信息；与居民合作，使用认知行为疗法、人际关系疗法和其他谈话疗法帮助他们了解他们的情绪问题 COUNSELLORS provide information on vocational, relationship, social and educational difficulties and issues, and work with people to help them to identify and define their emotional issues through therapies such as cognitive behaviour therapy, interpersonal therapy and other talking therapies.

ANZSCO 2721 顾问技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历（ANZSCO 1级水平）。 Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 2721 顾问主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 审核客户的能力、需求及相关信息，为客户提供职业、学习以及就业选择建议 working with clients on career, study and employment options by obtaining and examining information relevant to their abilities and needs
• 提供信息与资源，协助客户提高求职技巧 providing information and resources to assist clients with job-seeking skills
• 评估与滥用毒品和酗酒相关的治疗需求 assessing client needs in relation to treatment for drug and alcohol abuse
• 为个人、夫妻和家庭提供心理辅导 conducting counselling interviews with individuals, couples and family groups
• 协助客户调整态度、期望和行为，以发展更有效的人际和婚姻关系 assisting the understanding and adjustment of attitudes, expectations and behaviour to develop more effective interpersonal and marital relationships
• 提出多种态度和行为的变换方法，并讨论它们的可行性 presenting alternative approaches and discussing potential for attitude and behaviour change
• 与客户协商制定康复计划，考虑到职业、社会、医疗和心理需求 consulting with clients to develop rehabilitation plans taking account of vocational and social needs
• 对学生，特别是那些有特殊需求的学生的学习和行为提供信息和建议，并协助老师家长处理这些需求 contributing information, understanding and advice on the learning and behaviour of students, especially those with special needs, and assisting parents and teachers in dealing with these needs
• 可能在呼叫中心工作 may work in a call centre
