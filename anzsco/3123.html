ANZSCO 3123 电气工程绘图员和技术员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 准备电气装置和电路系统的绘图，设计和图表 preparing drawings, plans and diagrams of electrical installations and circuitry
• 协助电气工程师和工程技师设计和规划电气装置，变电站的电路系统，开关设备，电缆系统和电动机控制系统 assisting Electrical Engineers and Engineering Technologists in design and layout of electrical installations and circuitry on substations, switchgear, cabling systems and motor control systems
• 收集资料，进行测试和复杂的计算，绘制结果并准备图表和表格 collecting data, performing tests and complex calculations, graphing results, and preparing charts and tabulations
• 估算材料的成本和数量 estimating materials costs and quantities
• 检测设计和已完成产品是否符合规格和规范 inspecting designs and finished products for compliance with specifications and regulations
• 装配，安装，测试，校正，调整和维修电气设备和装置，以确保其符合规章和安全要求 assembling, installing, testing, calibrating, modifying and repairing electrical equipment and installations to conform with regulations and safety requirements
• 发挥电气车间功能，如为保护继电器安装配件，测量并指示设备 undertaking electrical workshop functions such as installing assemblies for protection relays, metering and indicating devices
• 协助研究与实验工程 assisting with research and experimentation programs
