ANZSCO 4117 福利人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 向客户提供情感、金钱、娱乐、健康、住房和其它社会福利方面的、信息和建议，评估和协调福利和社区服务机构的服务。WELFARE SUPPORT WORKERS provide support, information and advice to clients on emotional, financial, recreational, health, housing and other social welfare matters, and evaluate and coordinate the services of welfare and community service agencies.

ANZSCO 4117 福利人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 评估客户需求，规划、发展和执行教育、培训和项目。assessing clients’ needs and planning, developing and implementing educational, training and support programs
• 访问客户，评估困难的实质和范围。interviewing clients and assessing the nature and extent of difficulties
• 监控和报告客户的进展。 monitoring and reporting on the progress of clients
• 向客户建议能提供额外帮助的机构。referring clients to agencies that can provide additional help
• 评估健康、福利、住房、就业、培训和其它设施和服务的社区需求和资源。assessing community need and resources for health, welfare, housing, employment, training and other facilities and services
• 与社区组织、福利机构、政府机构和私营企业共同处理社区事宜，提升社区资源和服务的意识。liaising with community groups, welfare agencies, government bodies and private businesses about community issues and promoting awareness of community resources and services
• 支持家庭，为儿童和残疾人提供教育和关爱。supporting families and providing education and care for children and disabled persons in adult service units, group housing and government institutions
• 监管缓刑和假释人员。 supervising offenders on probation and parole
• 协助年轻人解决社会、情感和经济上的困境。 assisting young people to solve social, emotional and financial problems
• 准备资金和资源方面的意见书，并向政府机构和其它机构报告。preparing submissions for funding and resources, and reports to government bodies and other agencies
