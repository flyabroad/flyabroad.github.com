ANZSCO 2223 金融投资顾问和管理人主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 对潜力客户进行面试以确定其财政地位与目标，讨论期权，金融计划与投资策略。interviewing prospective clients to determine financial status and objectives, discussing financial options and developing financial plans and investment strategies
• 监督投资效益，基于市场需要与变化修订投资计划 monitoring investment performance, and reviewing and revising investment plans based on modified needs and changes in markets
• 为客户推荐，安排保险范围 recommending and arranging insurance cover for clients
• 为客户买卖股票与债券 arranging to buy and sell stocks and bonds for clients
• 建议投资计划，资金来源，收入分配 advising on investment strategies, sources of funds and the distribution of earnings
• 设置财政目标并执行可达到财政目标的计划 setting financial objectives, and developing and implementing strategies for achieving the financial Objectives
• 管理个人退休金与单位信托基金 managing funds raised from personal superannuation savings policies and unit trusts
• 协助满足退休金要求 assisting in meeting superannuation compliance requirements
• 指导财政，会计，投资信息的收集；报表，预算的准备等 directing the collection of financial, accounting and investment information and the preparation of budgets, reports, forecasts and statutory returns
• 指引客户到其他组织以获取财政计划内服务 may refer clients to other organisations to obtain services outlined in financial plans
