ANZSCO 1494 运输服务经理 Transport Services Managers - FLYabroad

运输服务经理负责租赁公司车辆的购买和销售，协调车辆出租、火车站运营以及用车辆运送商品和乘客的公司的运营。 Transport Services Managers organise and control the buying and selling of vehicles for rental agencies and coordinate the leasing of vehicles, the operations of railway stations, and the operations of enterprises that operate fleets of vehicles to transport goods and passengers.

可能需要注册或许可。 Registration or licensing may be required.

ANZSCO 1494 运输服务经理主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 安排运输车辆、设备和燃料的购买和维护 organising the purchase and maintenance of transport vehicles, equipment and fuel
• 联络客户，群定客户需求，并在车辆类型、购买和租用价格和义务、投诉方面为客户提供建议和信息。liaising with clients to determine requirements and providing customers with advice and information regarding vehicle type, purchase or hire rates and obligations and handling complaints
• 接收订单和预定，制定和实施运输行程表 receiving orders and bookings, and planning and implementing transportation schedules
• 保证商品良好的存储和运输 ensuring goods are stored and transported in conditions that will maintain their quality
• 安排车辆和商品的上门取送 arranging collection and delivery of vehicles and goods
• 维持业务记录，准备运营报表和报告 maintaining business records and preparing operational statements and reports
• 协调火车的到站、运输以及装卸工作 coordinating activities associated with the arrival, departure, loading and unloading of trains
• 遵守职业健康和安全条例 ensuring compliance with occupational health and safety regulations
