ANZSCO 322 制造工程工人职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 制造工程工人连接、成型、塑造金属。板金工、车身制造工并未包含在此组，它们在324组中。FABRICATION ENGINEERING TRADES WORKERS cast, shape, cut, join and finish metal. Panel Beaters and Vehicle Body Builders are excluded from this minor group. Panel Beaters and Vehicle Body Builders are included in Minor Group 324 Panelbeaters, and Vehicle Body Builders, Trimmers and Painters.

ANZSCO 322 制造工程工人技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本职业需要如下条件：Most occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below.
• 在澳大利亚： In Australia:
• AQF3级证书加2年以上在职培训或AQF4级证书（ANZSCO 3级水平）。 AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰： In New Zealand:
• NZ4级注册证书（ANZSCO 3级水平）。NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 三年以上相关经验可以代替上方所示正规资格。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和/或在职培训经历。需要注册或许可证。 At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. Registration or licensing may be required.

ANZSCO 322 制造工程工人主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 研究模板、图纸与说明书以确定要求与金属材料 studying blueprints, drawings and specifications to determine requirements and material, and selecting metal stock
• 铸造熔融金属，保护性和装饰材料五金制品 moulding molten metal, and applying protective and decorative finishes to metal products
• 喷漆、标志款式 drawing and marking out patterns
• 加热金属原料，用锤子定型 heating metal stock and shaping it using hammers
• 手工将原料切割、定型等 cutting stock and moulding components to shape using hand tools, cutting torches and machine tools
• 通过焊接、铜焊、铆接等方式连接部件 joining components by welding, soldering, brazing, riveting, bolting, screwing and gluing
• 通过清洁、锉削、抛光、喷涂保护涂层来完成产品 finishing products by cleaning, filing, sanding and polishing, and applying protective finishes
