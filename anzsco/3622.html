ANZSCO 3622 园丁 GARDENERS - FLYabroad

ANZSCO 3622 园丁职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 种植、培养、维护、设计和建造公园、花园和景观，检查、判断和处理树木和灌木。GARDENERS plant, cultivate, maintain, plan and construct parks, gardens and landscapes, and inspect, diagnose and treat trees and shrubs

ANZSCO 3622 园丁技能要求 Indicative 技术等级 Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本单元的大多数职位需要具有与下述资质和经验相当的技术层次。Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with the qualifications and experience outlined below
• 在澳洲：AQF证书3级，包括至少2年的在职培训或AQF证书IV级（ANZSCO技术3级）In Australia: AQF Certificate III including at least two years of on-the-job training, or AQF Certificate IV (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 在新西兰：NNZ资格证4级（ANZSCO技术3级）In New Zealand: NZ Register Level 4 qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 3)
• 至少3年的工作经验可以代替上述的资格证。At least three years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualifications listed above.
• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3622 园丁主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 准备和维护温床和生长点。preparing and maintaining seedbeds and growing sites
• 种植和陪护树木、灌木、树篱、花和鳞茎。propagating and planting trees, bushes, hedges, flowers and bulbs
• 通过散开表层土、种草和铺设即时草坪，准备草地区域。preparing lawn areas by spreading top soil and planting grass, and by laying instant turf
• 通过除草、修边、施肥、浇水和割草来维护植被和草地。maintaining planted and grassed areas by weeding, trimming, fertilising, watering and mowing
• 修剪树木和树篱，安装植被辅助和保护设备。pruning trees and hedges, and installing plant support and protection devices
• 准备方案和图纸，筛选材料和植物并为景观建设作出计划。preparing plans and drawings, selecting materials and plants, and scheduling landscape construction
• 安装软硬景观结构。setting out and installing hardscape and softscape structures
• 构造已铺筑的地区、墙壁、栅栏、藤架、池塘、烧烤和花园家具。constructing gravel and paved areas, walls, fences, pergolas, ponds, barbecues and garden furniture
• 检查树木，对情况作出评估并确定治疗方案。examining trees to assess their condition and determine treatment
• 使用链条和手锯剪枝和修型。lopping limbs off trees and shaping branches using chain and handsaws
• 对植物和树木喷药来控制病虫害并砍掉病树。spraying and dusting plants and trees to control insects and disease, and felling diseased trees
