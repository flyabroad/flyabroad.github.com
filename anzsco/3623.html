ANZSCO 3623 球场管理人职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 建立和维护草坪、草地和用于体育赛事的人造场地。GREENKEEPERS establish and maintain fine turf, grassed areas and synthetic surfaces used for sporting events

ANZSCO 3623 球场管理人主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 为新草坪准备苗床。preparing seedbeds for new turf
• 通过浇水和播种建立和维护草坪，修复绿地破坏。establishing and maintaining turf by watering, over sowing or over seeding, and repairing green damage
• 割草、轧制和校平。mowing, rolling and levelling turf
• 固定和标出界线和标识语，安装网、标杆和立柱，并放置其它的设备。pegging and marking out lines and logos, installing nets, posts and stumps, and placing other sports equipment on playing areas
• 操作和维护手动和动力驱动设备，如割草机、打孔机、除草机、去心器和标线设备。operating and maintaining hand and power driven equipment such as mowers, aerators, cultivators, corers and line marking equipment
• 构造板球边门，网球场，保龄球、槌球和高尔夫球场。constructing cricket wickets, tennis courts, and bowling, croquet and golf greens
• 对草地重植、修理、充气、施肥和根外追肥。replanting, repairing, aerating, fertilising and top dressing lawns
• 安装和维护人造场地。installing and maintaining synthetic surfaces
• 可能需要维护建筑、栅栏和周围的花园。may maintain buildings, fences and surrounding gardens
