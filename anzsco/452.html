ANZSCO 452 体育和健身人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 体育和健身人员指导个人和团体进行体育锻炼和户外活动，指导体育比赛，参加或主持体育节目。SPORTS AND FITNESS WORKERS direct, instruct and guide individuals and groups in physical fitness and outdoor adventure activities, coach, train and instruct sporting competitors, and participate in and officiate at sporting events.

ANZSCO 452 体育和健身人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 开发和设计健身项目。 developing and designing fitness programs
• 在各种健身活动中，采用小组练习和个人授课的方式。delivering group exercise classes and personal tuition in a variety of fitness activities
• 组织和管理参加户外冒险的小组，如蹦极、垂钓和捕猎、登山、徒步旅行和漂流。organising and supervising groups involved in outdoor adventures such as bungy jumping, fishing and hunting, mountaineering, trekking and whitewater rafting
• 对参赛要用到的户外探险设备和技巧提供指导。demonstrating and providing instruction in the use of outdoor adventure equipment and techniques required for participation
• 通过分析表现和发展能力来辅导、培训和指导运动员。coaching, training and instructing sportspersons by analysing performances and developing abilities
• 规划和指导比赛策略，发展比赛模式，分析比赛进展和鼓励运动员。planning and directing game strategies, developing play patterns, analysing game progress and motivating players
• 促进体育和体育技能发展，并监管年轻运动员。promoting sports and sports skills development, and overseeing the participation of young people in sport
• 在体育赛事中担任裁判。officiating at sporting events to enforce rules
• 协调和指导体育活动，与其他裁判一起解释和执行规章制度。coordinating and directing sporting activities, and liaising with other officials to interpret and enforce rules and regulations relating to sport
• 参加比赛并接受培训。competing in and training for sporting events
