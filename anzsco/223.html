ANZSCO 223 人力资源培训专员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 人力资源与培训专员计划执行员工招聘，培训，开发方案，协助解决纠纷，对劳资关系的政策和问题提供意见，代表工业，商业，工会和雇主或其他各方参加协商。HUMAN RESOURCE AND TRAINING PROFESSIONALS plan, develop, implement and evaluate staff recruitment, retention, training and development programs, assist in resolving disputes by advising on workplace relations policies and problems, and represent industrial, commercial, union, employer and other parties in negotiations.
