ANZSCO 2345 生命科学家职业描述 - FLYabroad

• 生命科学家检测人类，动物，植物和其他有机体的解剖学，生理学和生态化学，以更好的了解他们的功能和他们是怎样与所生存的环境相互作用的 LIFE SCIENTISTS examine the anatomy, physiology and biochemistry of humans, animals, plants and other living organisms to better understand how living organisms function and interact with each other and the environment in which they live.

• 此类别的大多数职业要求相应的本科或更高资格。某些情况下，除正规学历外需要相关的工作经验或在职培训。（ANZSCO 技术水平 1级）Most occupations in this unit group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition
to the formal qualification (ANZSCO Skill Level 1).

ANZSCO 2345 生命科学家主要职责 - FLYabroad

• 设计并指导实验，做观察和测试，研究信息，分析数据，准备和监督实验室报告和科学论文，在科学会议和研讨会上展示发现，监督职员的工作 designing and conducting experiments, making observations and measurements, researching information,analysing data, preparing or supervising the preparation of laboratory reports and scientific papers,presenting findings at scientific meetings and conferences, and supervising the work of staff
• 通过系统观察，解剖和微观检测研究身体器官和组织的形式和结构 studying the forms and structures of bodily organs and tissues by systematic observation, dissection and microscopic examination
• 研究活细胞的结构和功能，并研究人类，动物，植物和微生物相对独立的组成部分，器官和组织 investigating the chemical structure and function of living cells and their isolated components, organs and tissues in humans, animals, plants, and micro-organisms
• 检验微生物，例如，细菌，真菌，酵母和酶，利用所学知识发展新的提高现有的产品，材料和程序 examining micro-organisms, such as bacteria, fungi, yeast and their enzymes, and using the knowledge gained to create and develop new, and improve existing, products, materials and processes
• 研究环境因素对植物生长的影响，例如降雨量，气温，阳光，土壤，地形和疾病investigating the effects of environmental factors, such as rainfall, temperature, sunlight, soil, topography and disease, on plant growth
• 计划并进行试验来研究，测试和了解海洋动物和植物 planning and undertaking experiments to study, measure and understand marine animals and plants
• 研究微生物的生长和特征，例如，细菌，藻类和真菌，和他们对植物，动物和人类的影响，发展医学的，兽医的，工业的，环境的和其他实际的应用 studying the growth and characteristics of micro-organisms, such as bacteria, algae and fungi, and the effects they have on plants, animals and humans to develop medical, veterinary, industrial, environmental and other practical applications
• 调查动物们在自然环境，被关起来的环境和在实验室中的相互关系 investigating the interrelationships between animals in their natural surroundings, in captivity and in laboratories
