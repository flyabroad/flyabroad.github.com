ANZSCO 241 学校教师职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 学校教师在幼儿（学前）教育、小学、初中、高中以及特殊教育机构教授一系列必修课程；促进学生的社会，情绪，智力以及体质发展 SCHOOL TEACHERS educate students in early childhood (pre-primary), primary, middle or intermediate, secondary and special institutions by teaching a range of subjects within a prescribed curriculum, and promote students' social, emotional, intellectual and physical development.

ANZSCO 241 学校教师主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 选择、准备并讲授适合学生水平的课程和教材 selecting, preparing and presenting courses, teaching materials and lessons suited to the abilities of the students
• 修改并评估学生的作业 correcting and assessing students' work
• 致力于学生的社交、情绪、智力及体质健康，并维护可接受的行为规范 attending to the social, emotional, intellectual and physical wellbeing of students and maintaining acceptable standards of behaviour
• 通过亲身的观察和考试评估学生成绩和能力 assessing the abilities and level of achievement of students through direct observation and administering tests
• 与其他专业人员、家长以及学生讨论个人进步问题 discussing individual progress and problems with other professionals, parents and students
• 执行课外任务，比如协助组织运动会、音乐会、郊游等趣味活动 performing extra-curricular tasks such as assisting with sport, school concerts, excursions and special interest programs
• 管理实习老师 supervising student teachers on placement
• 记录考勤并维持学校纪律 maintaining attendance and scholastic records, and school discipline
