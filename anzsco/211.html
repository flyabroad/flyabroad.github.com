ANZSCO 211 艺术类专业人员职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 艺术类专业人员通过媒体传达想法、观念和事实真相以达到特定的效果，诠释艺术作品如音乐曲谱和手稿。
ARTS PROFESSIONALS communicate ideas, impressions and facts in a range of media to achieve particular effects, and interpret compositions such as a musical scores and scripts for performance.

ANZSCO 211 艺术类专业人员技能要求 Indicative Skill Level - FLYabroad

• 本组中大多数职业都要求具有与学士学位或更高资格相当的技能水平。五年以上相关工作经验可以代替正规资格要求。某些情况下，除了要求有正规资格还要求有相关经验和在职培训经历。这一职业需要很高的创作才华、个人对工作的执着和兴趣，或有任职资格或经验。（ANZSCO 1级水平） In Australia and New Zealand:Most occupations in this minor group have a level of skill commensurate with a bachelor degree or higher qualification. At least five years of relevant experience may substitute for the formal qualification. In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification. Some occupations in this minor group require high levels of creative talent or personal commitment and interest as well as, or in place of, formal qualifications or experience (ANZSCO Skill Level1).

ANZSCO 211 艺术类专业人员主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 构思和拓展想法、风格、个性、情感和主题并将它们通过艺术方式展现conceiving and developing ideas, styles, characters, emotions and themes and translating them through artistic presentation
• 阅读剧本并进行研究，做到对其角色、主题和角色个性塑造充分了解 reading scripts and undertaking research to gain understanding of parts, themes and characterisations
• 作曲和编舞并创造新的视觉艺术形式，如绘画、摄影和雕塑 composing music and dance routines and creating visual forms such as paintings, pictures and sculptures
• 了解台词、角色、线索、舞蹈动作和音乐 learning lines, parts, cues, dance routines and music
• 表演音乐、舞蹈、戏剧角色和其他的观众娱乐形式 performing music, dances, dramatic roles and other parts for audience entertainment
• 运输和安装摄影设备并拍摄照片 transporting and setting up photographic equipment, and taking photographs
