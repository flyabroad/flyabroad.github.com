ANZSCO 3132 通讯技术专家职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 通讯技术专家为通讯网络开发，监测并执行技术支持功能；安装计算机设备，微波，遥测技术，多重发讯，卫星以及其其他无线电和电磁波通讯系统 TELECOMMUNICATIONS TECHNICAL SPECIALISTS develop, monitor and carry out technical support functions for telecommunications networks and install computer equipment, computer systems and microwave, telemetry, multiplexing, satellite and other radio and electromagnetic wave communication systems.

ANZSCO 3132 通讯技术专家主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 安装，维护，维修并诊断微波，遥测技术，多重发讯，卫星以及其他无线电和电磁波通讯系统 installing, maintaining, repairing and diagnosing malfunctions of microwave, telemetry, multiplexing, satellite and other radio and electromagnetic wave communication systems
• 配置并整合网络通讯技术，包括软件，硬件，桌面，周边设备，数据库以及操作系统configuring and integrating network and telecommunications technology with computer software, hardware, desktops, peripherals, databases and operating systems
• 开发并记录库存，零件和机械设备的详细资料和位置；维护通讯政策，程序，指导方针和规章以及质量标准等相关文件材料 developing and recording logs of the details, locations and status of inventories, parts, equipment and instruments and maintaining the documentation of communication policies, procedures, guidelines and regulations, and quality standards
• 提供技术方面的建议与信息并监测较复杂的通讯网络和设备的性能 providing technical advice and information, and monitoring the performance of complex telecommunications networks and equipment
• 规划客户通讯网络接收基础设施的开发 planning the development of customer access telecommunications network infrastructure
• 与供应商，服务提供商以及外部资源保持联系；监测合同义务以及履行情况 liaising with vendors, suppliers, service providers and external resources and monitoring contractual obligations and performance delivery
• 在通讯网络的设计，优化，故障排除，诊断维修以及性能故障解决方面提供持续的操作支持 providing ongoing operational support in designing, optimising, troubleshooting, diagnosing, repairing and resolving of telecommunications network performance malfunctions, defects and faults
