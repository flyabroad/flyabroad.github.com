ANZSCO 3424 电信行业技工职责概述 - FLYabroad

• 安装、维护和修理数据传输设备、天线、导电管、电缆、无线电天线、电信设备和电器。TELECOMMUNICATIONS TRADES WORKERS install, maintain and repair data transmission equipment, aerial lines, conduits, cables, radio antennae and telecommunications equipment and appliances

• 有时除了资格证外，还需要相关的工作经验和/或在职培训。In some instances relevant experience and/or on-the-job training may be required in addition to the formal qualification

ANZSCO 3424 电信行业技工主要职责 Tasks Include - FLYabroad

• 检查图纸、说明书和工作区以此确定要安装设备的位置和连接方式。examining drawings, specifications and work areas to determine positioning and connections for equipment to be installed
• 使用仪器（如欧姆表、电压表、电表和传送测量设备）定位设备的故障。locating faults in telecommunications equipment using instruments such as ohmmeters, voltmeters, ammeters and transmission measuring equipment
• 将电线和电缆与电器进行连接。attaching wires and cables to appliances
• 校正、更换和修理故障产品，使用电子仪器测试设备。adjusting, replacing and repairing faulty items, and testing equipment using electronic instruments
• 为电话、收音机、付费电视和计算机传输安装电缆。installing cabling for telephone, radio, pay TV and computer transmission
• 使用导线和热塑塑料连接电缆的密封套。joining cables and sealing sheaths with lead and thermoplastic
• 安装、测试和维护天线和地下电缆、无线电天线和手机天线。erecting, testing and maintaining aerial and underground wires and cables, and radio and mobile phone antennae
• 安装电信设备和电器，如电话、配电盘和数据传输设备。installing telecommunications equipment and appliances such as telephones, switchboards and data transmission equipment
